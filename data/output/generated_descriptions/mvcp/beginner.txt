Minimum Vertex Cover Problem
The problem is about finding the smallest group of important points in a network that connects all the lines between them. 
The main variables are: 
- x, which shows whether a point is included in the group or not
- E, which represents the lines between the points
- w, which is the weight or importance of each point
- z, which is the total weight of the points in the group
The basic restrictions are: 
- each line must have at least one of its endpoints in the group
The goal is to minimize the total weight of the points in the group, which means finding the smallest and most efficient group of points that connects all the lines.
