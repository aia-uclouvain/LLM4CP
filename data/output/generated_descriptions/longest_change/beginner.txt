The problem is called Longest Change. 
It's about finding the longest sequence of numbers where each number is different from the one before it. 
The main variables are x, which is a list of numbers, and c, which is the length of the longest sequence of different numbers. 
The basic restrictions are that the numbers in x must be between 1 and 8, and that the sequence must be as long as possible.
