xkcd Knapsack problem
The problem is about ordering food from a menu to get a total bill of exactly $15.05. 
The main variables are x, which represents how many items of each dish to order, and y, which represents the number of different dishes ordered.
The basic restrictions are that the total cost of the ordered food must be exactly $15.05, and the number of items of each dish must be a whole number.
The goal of the problem is to maximize the number of different dishes ordered, which means finding the combination of dishes that gives a total bill of $15.05 while ordering the most variety of dishes.
