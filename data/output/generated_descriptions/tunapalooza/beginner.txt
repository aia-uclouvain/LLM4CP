Tunapalooza 
The problem is about two friends, Tim and Keri, who are attending a music concert called Tunapalooza. They want to see four different rock bands, but they will split up to cover more ground. They have planned to meet at specific points during each band's performance. The task is to figure out which type of music each band plays and where Tim and Keri will meet during each performance.

The main variables are the names of the bands (Ellyfish, Korrupt, Retread Ed and the Flat Tires, and Yellow Reef), the type of music each band plays (country, grunge, reggae, or speed metal), and the meeting points (carnival games, information booth, mosh pit, or T-shirt vendor).

The basic restrictions or rules are: 
- Korrupt does not play country or grunge music.
- Tim and Keri will not meet at the carnival games during Ellyfish's performance.
- They will not meet at the T-shirt vendor during the reggae band's show.
- Exactly two of the following statements are true: Ellyfish plays grunge music, Tim and Keri will not meet at the information booth during Retread Ed and the Flat Tires' performance, and they will not meet at the T-shirt vendor during Yellow Reef's performance.
- The country and speed metal bands are either Retread Ed and the Flat Tires or the band playing when Tim and Keri meet at the mosh pit.
- The reggae band is neither Korrupt nor the band playing when Tim and Keri meet at the information booth.

The goal of the problem is to determine the type of music each band plays and where Tim and Keri will meet during each performance, given the restrictions and rules provided.
