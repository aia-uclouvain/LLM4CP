name: Tunapalooza Puzzle
description: The problem is about matching four rock bands (Ellyfish, Korrupt, Retread Ed and the Flat Tires, and Yellow Reef) with the type of music they play (country, grunge, reggae, or speed metal) and the prearranged meeting spot of two friends, Tim and Kerri, during their performances at the Tunapalooza '98 concert.
variables: $country, grunge, reggae, speed\_metal, carnival\_games, information\_booth, mosh\_pit, T\_shirt\_vendor$ - These variables represent the decision variables in the problem, where $country, grunge, reggae, speed\_metal$ are the music genres assigned to each band, and $carnival\_games, information\_booth, mosh\_pit, T\_shirt\_vendor$ are the meeting spots assigned to each band's performance.
constraints: $all\_different(genre) \wedge all\_different(rendevouz) \wedge Korrupt \neq country \wedge Korrupt \neq grunge \wedge Ellyfish \neq carnival\_games \wedge reggae \neq T\_shirt\_vendor \wedge (bool2int(Ellyfish = grunge) + bool2int(information\_booth \neq Retread\_Ed\_and\_the\_Flat\_Tires) + bool2int(T\_shirt\_vendor \neq Yellow\_Reef)) = 2 \wedge ((country = Retread\_Ed\_and\_the\_Flat\_Tires \wedge speed\_metal = mosh\_pit) \vee (speed\_metal = Retread\_Ed\_and\_the\_Flat\_Tires \wedge country = mosh\_pit)) \wedge reggae \neq Korrupt \wedge reggae \neq information\_booth$ - These constraints ensure that each band is assigned a unique music genre and meeting spot, and that the given conditions are satisfied, such as Korrupt not playing country or grunge music, and the reggae band not meeting at the T-shirt vendor.
objective: The objective of the problem is to find a satisfying assignment of music genres and meeting spots to the bands, with no specific value to minimize or maximize, as it is a constraint satisfaction problem.
