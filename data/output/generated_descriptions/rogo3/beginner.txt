Rogo problem
The Rogo problem is about finding a path in a grid that collects as many points as possible. 
The main variables are the x and y coordinates of each step in the path, and the points collected at each step. 
The basic restrictions are that the path must be of a fixed length, it cannot visit the same cell twice, and it cannot go through black cells. 
The goal is to maximize the total number of points collected.
