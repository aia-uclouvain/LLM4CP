Pairwise Sum Problem
The problem is about identifying individual numbers from their pairwise sums, given in non-decreasing order. 
The main decision variables are the array `x`, which represents the individual numbers. 
The key constraints are that each pairwise sum of the individual numbers must match the given input sums, and the individual numbers must be in non-increasing order. 
The problem's objective is to find the individual numbers that satisfy these constraints, with no explicit minimization or maximization objective, but rather a satisfaction of the given conditions.
