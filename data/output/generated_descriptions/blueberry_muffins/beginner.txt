Blueberry Muffins problem
The problem is about figuring out who ate how many blueberry muffins in a house with four people. 
The main variables are the first names, last names, jobs, and number of muffins each person ate. 
The basic restrictions are that each person ate a different number of muffins, and there are specific clues about who ate how many, such as Bill and Ellis together ate six muffins. 
The goal is to determine each person's full name, job, and number of muffins they ate.
