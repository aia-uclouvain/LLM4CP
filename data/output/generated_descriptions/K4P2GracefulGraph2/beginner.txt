K4P2 Graceful Graph problem. 
This problem is about finding a way to label the nodes and edges of two specific graphs, K4 and P2, in a special way. 
The main variables are nodes, which represent the labels of the points in the graphs, and edges, which represent the labels of the connections between these points. 
The basic restrictions are that each node and each edge must have a unique label, and the difference between the labels of two nodes connected by an edge must be equal to the label of that edge. 
The goal of the problem is to find a set of labels for the nodes and edges that satisfy these restrictions, which is known as a "graceful" labeling of the graphs.
