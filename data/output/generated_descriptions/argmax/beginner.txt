The problem is called argmax/argmin predicate. 
It's about finding the position of the largest and smallest values in a list of numbers, and making sure the list follows certain rules. 
The main variables are x, which is a list of numbers, and pos, which is the position of the largest value in the list. 
The basic restrictions are that all numbers in the list must be different, the largest value must be in a specific position, and the smallest value must be in another specific position. 
The goal of the problem is to find a list of numbers that satisfies all these rules.
