Post Office Problem
The problem is about a post office that needs to determine the minimum number of full-time employees to hire in order to meet its daily requirements, given that each full-time employee works for 5 consecutive days and then takes 2 days off.

The main decision variables are represented by the array `x`, where `x[i]` denotes the number of workers starting at day `i`. These variables represent the number of employees that start working on each day of the week.

The key constraints are:
- Each full-time employee works for 5 consecutive days and then takes 2 days off.
- The post office must meet its daily requirements, which vary from 11 to 19 workers per day.
- The number of workers starting at each day must be non-negative.

The objective of the problem is to minimize the total number of employees that must be hired, represented by the variable `z`, which is the sum of the number of workers starting at each day. In other words, the post office wants to find the smallest number of full-time employees that can cover all the daily requirements while following the union rules.
