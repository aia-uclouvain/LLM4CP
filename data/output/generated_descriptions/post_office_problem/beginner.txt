Post Office Problem
The problem is about a post office that needs to hire full-time employees to work on different days of the week. 
The main variables are the number of workers needed each day and the number of workers starting on each day. 
The basic restrictions are that each full-time employee must work for 5 consecutive days and then have 2 days off, and the post office must meet its daily worker requirements. 
The goal is to minimize the total number of employees that must be hired.
