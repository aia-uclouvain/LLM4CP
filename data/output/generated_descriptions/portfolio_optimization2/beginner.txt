Portfolio Optimization Problem
The problem is about investing money in different shares to get the best possible return, while following some rules to minimize risk.
The main variables are: 
- SHARES: a list of different shares to invest in
- frac: the percentage of money to invest in each share
- return: the total return on investment
- ret: the estimated return for each share
The basic restrictions or rules are: 
- not investing more than a third of the money in high-risk shares
- investing at least half of the money in shares from North America
- investing all the available money
- not investing more than 30% of the money in any single share
The goal of the problem is to maximize the total return on investment.
