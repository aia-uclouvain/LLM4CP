Transshipment problem
The problem is about transporting goods from some cities to other cities in the most cost-effective way. 
The main variables are: 
- cities: the places where goods are sent from or received
- cost: how much it costs to transport goods between two cities
- supply: how much goods each city has to send
- demand: how much goods each city needs to receive
- delivery: how much goods are actually transported between two cities
The basic restrictions or rules are: 
- each city can only send as much goods as it has
- each city must receive at least as much goods as it needs
The goal of the problem is to minimize the total cost of transporting goods between cities.
