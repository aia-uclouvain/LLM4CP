Full Adder problem. 
This problem is about designing a simple electronic circuit that can add two binary numbers (which are either 0 or 1) and a carry from a previous addition, and produce a result and a new carry. 
The main variables are I1, I2, and I3, which represent the two binary numbers and the carry, and O1 and O2, which represent the result and the new carry. 
The basic restrictions are that the circuit must follow the rules of binary addition, which means that when you add two numbers and a carry, you get a result and a new carry. 
The goal of the problem is to find values for I1 and I2 that satisfy these rules, given that I3 (the carry) is 0 and O2 (the new carry) is 1.
