Freight Transfer problem. 
This problem is about transporting a certain amount of goods (42 tons of freight) using a fleet of trucks that come in different sizes and have different costs. 
The main variables are the number of trucks to use of each size (x), the total cost (z), and the total amount of goods transported (transported). In simple terms, x means how many trucks of each size to use, z is the total cost of using these trucks, and transported is the total amount of goods moved. 
The basic restrictions are that we can only use a certain number of trucks of each size (available), each truck can only carry a certain amount of goods (capacity), and we must transport at least the required amount of goods (42 tons). 
The goal of the problem is to minimize the total cost (z) of transporting the required amount of goods.
