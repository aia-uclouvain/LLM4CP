Kakuro puzzle
The problem is about filling in numbers in a grid to meet certain rules. Imagine you have a puzzle with some blank spaces and some clues. The goal is to fill in the blank spaces with numbers from 1 to 9 so that the sum of the numbers in each row and column matches the given clues, and no number is repeated in any row or column.
The main variables are the blank spaces in the grid, which are represented by the variable x. Each x[i,j] represents a blank space in the grid, where i is the row and j is the column. The hints_row and hints_col variables represent the clues for each row and column.
The basic restrictions are that each number can only be used once in each row and column, and the sum of the numbers in each row and column must match the given clues. There are also some blank spaces in the grid that are represented by the variable B, which means that those spaces are not part of any row or column clue.
The goal of the problem is to find a solution that satisfies all the rules and fills in all the blank spaces with numbers from 1 to 9. The solution is a completed grid where each row and column sum matches the given clues, and no number is repeated in any row or column.
