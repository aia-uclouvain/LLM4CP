name: Production Planning Problem
description: This problem involves planning the production of various materials, such as aluminum and silicon, and allocating them to different bins, while satisfying certain constraints and minimizing the total value of the production.
variables: $bin1, bin2, bin3, bin4, bin5, alum, silicon$ - These variables represent the quantities of materials allocated to each bin and the quantities of aluminum and silicon produced, respectively.
constraints: 
$\begin{aligned}
& alum \geq 0 \\
& silicon \geq 0 \\
& bin1 + bin2 + bin3 + bin4 + bin5 + alum + silicon = 2000 \\
& 0.15 \cdot bin1 + 0.04 \cdot bin2 + 0.02 \cdot bin3 + 0.04 \cdot bin4 + 0.02 \cdot bin5 + 0.01 \cdot alum + 0.03 \cdot silicon \leq 60 \\
& 0.03 \cdot bin1 + 0.05 \cdot bin2 + 0.08 \cdot bin3 + 0.02 \cdot bin4 + 0.06 \cdot bin5 + 0.01 \cdot alum \leq 100 \\
& 0.02 \cdot bin1 + 0.04 \cdot bin2 + 0.01 \cdot bin3 + 0.02 \cdot bin4 + 0.02 \cdot bin5 \leq 40 \\
& 0.02 \cdot bin1 + 0.03 \cdot bin2 + 0.01 \cdot bin5 \leq 30 \\
& 0.70 \cdot bin1 + 0.75 \cdot bin2 + 0.80 \cdot bin3 + 0.75 \cdot bin4 + 0.80 \cdot bin5 + 0.97 \cdot alum \geq 1500 \\
& 250 \leq 0.02 \cdot bin1 + 0.06 \cdot bin2 + 0.08 \cdot bin3 + 0.12 \cdot bin4 + 0.02 \cdot bin5 + 0.01 \cdot alum + 0.97 \cdot silicon \\
& 0.02 \cdot bin1 + 0.06 \cdot bin2 + 0.08 \cdot bin3 + 0.12 \cdot bin4 + 0.02 \cdot bin5 + 0.01 \cdot alum + 0.97 \cdot silicon \leq 300
\end{aligned}$ 
- These constraints represent the limitations on the production quantities, such as non-negativity constraints, total material availability, and various material composition requirements.
objective: Minimize $0.03 \cdot bin1 + 0.08 \cdot bin2 + 0.17 \cdot bin3 + 0.12 \cdot bin4 + 0.15 \cdot bin5 + 0.21 \cdot alum + 0.38 \cdot silicon$ - The objective is to minimize the total value of the production, which is a weighted sum of the quantities of materials allocated to each bin and the quantities of aluminum and silicon produced.
