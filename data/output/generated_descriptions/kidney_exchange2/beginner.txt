Kidney Exchange Problem
The problem is about matching people who need a kidney transplant with compatible donors. 
The main variables are: 
- x: which kidney does person p get (or 0 if he/she gets no kidney)
- y: a matrix representing the exchange between people
- z: the total number of people who get a kidney
The basic restrictions are: 
- a person can only get a kidney from someone who is compatible with them
- if a person gets a kidney, the donor must also receive a kidney
- each person can only give one kidney and receive one kidney
The goal is to maximize the number of people who get a kidney, which means finding the largest possible number of compatible exchanges.
