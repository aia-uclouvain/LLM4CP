Maximum Subarray Problem
The problem is about finding a part of a list of numbers that adds up to the largest possible total. 
The main variables are: 
- a list of numbers (called "a" in the code), 
- the starting and ending positions of the part of the list we're interested in (called "from" and "to"), 
- the total sum of the numbers in that part of the list (called "tot_sum"), 
- and the answer, which is the part of the list that adds up to the largest total (stored in "ans").
The basic restrictions are: 
- we're only looking at a specific list of numbers, 
- the part of the list we choose must be a continuous sequence of numbers (i.e., we can't skip any numbers in the middle), 
- if all the numbers in the list are negative, the best we can do is choose an empty sequence (i.e., no numbers at all).
The goal is to maximize the total sum of the numbers in the chosen part of the list.
