Hidato Puzzle
The Hidato puzzle is a logic-based puzzle where you have to connect numbered tiles in a grid. 
The main variables are the grid size (number of rows and columns) and the values of the tiles in the grid. 
The basic restrictions are that each number can only be used once, and consecutive numbers must be placed in adjacent tiles (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally). 
The goal is to fill in the entire grid with consecutive numbers, starting from 1 and ending at the total number of tiles, in a way that satisfies the given restrictions.
