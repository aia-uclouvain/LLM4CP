Rock Start Dressing Problem
The problem is about a rock star who wants to choose an outfit that follows certain fashion rules. 
The main variables are the rock star's shirt, tie, and pants, which can be different colors. 
The basic restrictions are that certain colors of shirts, ties, and pants can or cannot be worn together. 
The goal of the problem is to find a combination of shirt, tie, and pants that satisfies all the fashion rules.
