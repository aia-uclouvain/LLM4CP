Bobsledders puzzle 
The problem is about figuring out which person is paired with whom, the color of the sled they use, and the country they represent in a bobsledding competition. 
The main variables are the names of the people, the colors of the sleds, and the countries they represent. In simple terms, the variables are: 
- The people (Zane, Bobby, Ted, Lance, Phil, Connie, Jane, Oprah, Betsy, Lulu) 
- The sled colors (Red, Blue, Black, Green, White) 
- The countries (Jamaica, Germany, Canada, USA, Romania) 
The basic restrictions or rules are: 
- Each country has a unique sled color 
- Each sled color is used by a different country 
- There are 5 teams, each with a male and a female 
- Certain people cannot be paired with each other or use certain sled colors 
The goal of the problem is to find out which person is paired with whom, the color of the sled they use, and the country they represent, while following the given rules and restrictions.
