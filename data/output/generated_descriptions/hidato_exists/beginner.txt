Hidato Puzzle
The Hidato puzzle is about connecting numbered tiles in a grid to form a continuous path. 
The main variables are the grid size (r and c) and the values of the tiles (x). 
The basic restrictions are that each number can only be used once, and consecutive numbers must be adjacent to each other (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally). 
The goal is to find a solution that connects all the numbers from 1 to the total number of tiles in the grid.
