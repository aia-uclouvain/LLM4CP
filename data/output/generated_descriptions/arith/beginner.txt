Name of the problem: Global Constraint Arith

The problem is about checking if a group of numbers follow a certain rule compared to another number. 

The main variables are: 
- x, which is a list of numbers that we are checking
- y, which is the number we are comparing x to

The basic restrictions or rules of the problem are: 
- All numbers in x must be between 1 and 7
- There are 5 numbers in x
- We are checking if all numbers in x are less than y

The goal of the problem is to check if all numbers in x are indeed less than y, which is 9 in this case, and to show the numbers in x and the value of y.
