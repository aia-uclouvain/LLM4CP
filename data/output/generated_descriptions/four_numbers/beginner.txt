The Four Number Problem. 
This problem is about finding three numbers that can be added together in different ways to get four other given numbers. 
The main variables are the four given numbers (called "r") and the three numbers we are trying to find (called "x"). 
The basic restrictions are that each of the three numbers must be between 1 and 10, and that the four given numbers can be made by adding different combinations of the three numbers. 
The goal is to find all possible sets of three numbers that satisfy these conditions, given a specific set of four numbers.
