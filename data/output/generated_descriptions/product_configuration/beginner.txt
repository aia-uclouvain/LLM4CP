Product Configuration
This problem is about designing a product by choosing the type and number of components to use. 
The main variables are the types of components (like power supply, disk drive, and memory chip) and how many of each to use. 
The basic restrictions are that each component type has a limited number of uses, and the total product must meet certain requirements like having enough disk space and memory. 
The goal is to minimize the total weight of the product while meeting these requirements.
