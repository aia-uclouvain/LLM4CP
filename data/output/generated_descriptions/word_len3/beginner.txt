Word Chain Problem. 
The problem is about finding a chain of words where each word starts with the last letter of the previous word. 
The main variables are the words in the given list and the chain of words that need to be found. 
The basic restrictions are that each word in the chain must start with the last letter of the previous word, and no word can be repeated in the chain. 
The goal is to find the longest possible chain of words that satisfy these conditions.
