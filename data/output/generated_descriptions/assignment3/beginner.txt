The Assignment Problem or Marriage Theorem. 
This problem is about matching people of opposite sexes to maximize overall happiness when each person spends all their time with one partner. 
The main variables are the people (men and women) and the time they spend together, with "x" representing whether a couple spends time together (1 for yes, 0 for no) and "cost" representing the happiness each couple would experience. 
The basic restrictions are that each person can only spend all their time with one person of the opposite sex. 
The goal is to maximize the total happiness of all couples, by finding the perfect matches between men and women.
