Capital Budgeting Problem
The problem is about deciding how to invest a limited amount of money in different projects to get the best return. 
The main variables are: 
- x, which represents whether a project is chosen (1) or not (0)
- npv, which is the profit or return of each project
- budget, which is the total amount of money available for investment
- cash_flow, which is the cost of each project
The basic restrictions are: 
- the total cost of chosen projects cannot exceed the available budget
- each project's cost must be non-negative
- the budget must be non-negative
The goal is to maximize the total return (z) by choosing the best combination of projects to invest in, given the limited budget and other constraints.
