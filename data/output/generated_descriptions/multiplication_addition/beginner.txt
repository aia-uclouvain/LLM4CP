Equation Puzzle
The problem is about finding different combinations of fractions that can be added together to get the same result as multiplying them together. 
The main variables are A, B, C, D, E, and F, which are numbers used to create fractions. 
The basic restrictions are that each fraction must be unique and that the first fraction must be less than the second, and the second must be less than the third. 
The goal is to find all possible combinations of these fractions where their sum equals their product.
