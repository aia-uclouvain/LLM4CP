Enigma problem. 
This problem is about a game where a person, Penny, thinks of four different positive digits and makes 24 different 4-digit numbers using these digits. She then adds up all these numbers and subtracts one of the numbers from the total. The result is a 6-digit number that starts with "122" and ends with "0". The goal is to find the 4-digit number that Penny subtracted from the total.

The main variables are the four digits chosen by Penny, the 24 numbers made from these digits, the sum of these 24 numbers, and the 6-digit result after subtracting one of the numbers. 

The basic restrictions are that the four digits must be different, the first digit cannot be zero, and the 6-digit result must start with "122" and end with "0". 

The goal is to find the 4-digit number that was subtracted from the total sum of the 24 numbers, which will give us the correct 6-digit result.
