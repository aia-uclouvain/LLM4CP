Team Picking Problem
The problem is about dividing a group of people into two teams in a fair way, considering their individual strengths or abilities. 
The main variables are the team each person belongs to (x) and the difference in total strength between the two teams (d). 
The basic restrictions are that each team must have the same number of people, and the teams are divided based on the strengths of the individuals. 
The goal is to minimize the difference in total strength between the two teams, making them as balanced as possible.
