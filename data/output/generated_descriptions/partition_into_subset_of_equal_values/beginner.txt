Partition Problem
The problem is about dividing a set of numbers into two or more groups, where the total sum of numbers in each group is the same.
The main variables are the numbers in the set (s) and the group each number belongs to (x). In simple terms, "s" is a list of numbers, and "x" is a list that says which group each number in "s" should be in.
The basic restrictions are that each number can only be in one group, and the total sum of numbers in each group must be equal. Think of it like dividing a bunch of coins into two piles, where the total value of coins in each pile is the same.
The goal is to find a way to divide the numbers into groups such that the sum of numbers in each group is equal, if possible.
