description: The problem involves finding a matrix of integers that satisfies the lexicographical ordering constraint, where both adjacent rows and adjacent columns are lexicographically ordered, meaning that they can be equal.

objective: The objective of the problem is to find a satisfying assignment, as indicated by the `solve satisfy` statement, meaning that the goal is to find a matrix that satisfies all the given constraints, without any specific minimization or maximization objective.
