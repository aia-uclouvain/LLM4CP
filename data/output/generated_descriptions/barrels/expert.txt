name: Barrels Problem
description: The problem is to fill in the missing digits on the sides of nine barrels with different digits from 1 to 6, such that each row and column contains each digit only once. The top of each barrel contains a number that is either the sum or product of the four different digits on the sides of the barrel. All numbers on the tops of the barrels must be different and less than 91.
variables: $n, m, t, barrels, ops, barrels\_op, contents, contents\_flat, mat, mat\_ix$ 
These variables represent the following: $n$ is the number of barrels, $m$ is the size of each barrel's contents, $t$ is the size of the Latin square, $barrels$ is an array of the numbers on the tops of the barrels, $ops$ is an array of the possible operations (sum or product), $barrels\_op$ is an array of the operations used for each barrel, $contents$ is a 3D array of the digits on the sides of the barrels, $contents\_flat$ is a 1D array of the digits on the sides of the barrels, $mat$ is a 2D array representing the Latin square, and $mat\_ix$ is a 2D array mapping the indices of $contents$ to $mat$.
constraints: $\forall b \in 1..n (all\_different([contents[b,i,j] | i,j \in 1..m]) \wedge ((product([contents[b,i,j] | i,j \in 1..m]) = barrels[b] \wedge barrels\_op[b] = 1) \vee (sum([contents[b,i,j] | i,j \in 1..m]) = barrels[b] \wedge barrels\_op[b] = 2))) \wedge all\_different(barrels) \wedge \forall i \in 1..t (all\_different([mat[i,j] | j \in 1..t]) \wedge all\_different([mat[j,i] | j \in 1..t])) \wedge \forall i,j \in 1..t (mat[i,j] = contents\_flat[mat\_ix[i,j]])$ 
These constraints ensure that: each barrel's contents are different, the number on the top of each barrel is either the sum or product of its contents, all numbers on the tops of the barrels are different, each row and column of the Latin square contains each digit only once, and the Latin square is correctly mapped from the barrel contents.
objective: satisfy 
The objective of the problem is to find a solution that satisfies all the constraints, with no specific value to minimize or maximize.
