Knapsack Problem
The problem is about finding the best way to pack two types of items, small and large, to get the maximum value while not exceeding certain limits.
The main variables are "smallset" and "largeset", which represent the number of small and large items to pack.
The basic restrictions are that we cannot pack a negative number of items, and we have limits on the total size of the items we can pack, which are represented by two equations: one for the total weight (200) and one for the total volume (160).
The goal of the problem is to maximize the total value, which is calculated as 5 times the number of small items plus 20 times the number of large items.
