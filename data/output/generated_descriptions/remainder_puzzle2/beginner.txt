Remainder Puzzle
The problem is about finding a number that meets certain conditions when divided by other numbers. 
The main variable is "x" which represents a list of numbers, where each number corresponds to the result of a division operation.
The basic restrictions are that when the number is divided by 3, 4, 5, and 6, it leaves remainders of 1, 2, 3, and 4, respectively.
The goal is to find a number that satisfies all these conditions, which can be thought of as solving a puzzle with multiple clues.
