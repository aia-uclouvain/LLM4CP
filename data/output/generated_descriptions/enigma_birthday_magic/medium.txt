The problem is called the "Birthday Magic" problem. 
It is about finding a person's birthdate and age, given a set of clues that form a magic square. 
The main decision variables are: x, a 2D array representing the magic square, age, the person's age, day, the day of the month of birth, month, the month of birth, year2, the last two digits of the year of birth, and year, the full year of birth. 
The key constraints are: the given numbers in the magic square must be satisfied, the sum of each row and column in the magic square must be equal to the age, the sum of the diagonals in the magic square must be equal to the age, and the calculated birth year plus the age must equal 2007. 
The problem's objective is to find a solution that satisfies all the constraints, which will give the person's birthdate and age.
