name: Birthday Magic
description: The problem is to find a person's birth date and age, given a set of clues that form a magic square. The magic square represents a 4x4 grid where each row, column, and diagonal sum up to the person's age. The top row of the grid contains the day, month, and year of birth, with the year split into two parts: the first two digits (19) and the last two digits. The goal is to find the birth date and age that satisfy all the given conditions.
variables: $x_{i,j}, age, day, month, year2, year$ where $x_{i,j}$ is a 4x4 matrix representing the magic square, $age$ is the person's age, $day$ and $month$ are the day and month of birth, $year2$ are the last two digits of the year of birth, and $year$ is the full year of birth. These variables represent the unknown values that need to be found to solve the problem.
constraints: $cp2d(x, array2d(1..n, 1..n, [...])) \wedge \forall k \in 1..n (\sum_{i=1}^{n} x_{k,i} = age \wedge \sum_{i=1}^{n} x_{i,k} = age) \wedge \sum_{i=1}^{n} x_{i,i} = age \wedge \sum_{i=1}^{n} x_{i,n-i+1} = age \wedge day = x_{1,1} \wedge month = x_{1,2} \wedge year2 = x_{1,4} \wedge year = 100*x_{1,3} + year2 \wedge year + age = 2007$ where the constraints ensure that the magic square is consistent, the row, column, and diagonal sums are equal to the age, and the birth date and age are correctly calculated.
