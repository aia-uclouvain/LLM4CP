Multiplicative Sequence Problem
The problem is about guessing a number in a long list of numbers that follows certain rules. 
The main variables are the numbers in the list, which are represented by "x" and are the values at each position in the list. 
The basic restrictions are that the list is strictly increasing (each number is greater than the previous one), the second number is 2, and when two positions in the list have numbers that are relatively prime (their greatest common divisor is 1), the value at the position that is the product of the two positions is the product of the values at those positions. 
The goal of the problem is to find the possible number that could be at the 1000th position in the list.
