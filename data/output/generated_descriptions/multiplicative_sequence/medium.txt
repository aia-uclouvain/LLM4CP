Multiplicative Sequence Problem
The problem is about finding a sequence of positive whole numbers that satisfies certain properties, specifically a multiplicative property where the value at the product of two relatively prime indices is the product of the values at those indices.
The main decision variables are the elements of the array `x`, where `x[i]` represents the `i`-th number in the sequence.
The key constraints are: 
- the sequence is strictly increasing, meaning each number is greater than the previous one, 
- the second number in the sequence is 2, 
- and the multiplicative property, which states that the value at the product of two relatively prime indices `i` and `j` is the product of the values at those indices, i.e., `x[i*j] = x[i]*x[j]`.
The problem's objective is to find a sequence that satisfies these constraints, specifically to determine the possible value(s) for the 1000th number in the sequence, `x[1000]`.
