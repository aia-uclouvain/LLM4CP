Arbitrage Loop Problem
The problem is about finding a way to make a profit by exchanging money between different currencies in a specific order, taking advantage of differences in exchange rates. 
The main variables are: 
- y, which represents the sequence of currencies to exchange, 
- profit, which represents the amount of money made at each exchange, and 
- z, which represents the total profit made after all exchanges. 
The basic restrictions are: 
- each currency can only be used once in the sequence, 
- the sequence must start and end with the same currency, and 
- the amount of money made at each exchange is determined by the exchange rate between the two currencies. 
The goal is to maximize the total profit, z, by finding the best sequence of currencies to exchange.
