Ormat Game
The Ormat game is about filling a square grid with numbers in such a way that certain rules are followed. 
The main variables are the numbers in each cell of the grid, which can be thought of as boxes that need to be filled with the right numbers.
The basic restrictions are that each row, column, and smaller square inside the grid must have a specific pattern of numbers. 
The goal is to find a way to fill the entire grid while following these rules, which is more of a puzzle to be solved rather than something to be minimized or maximized.
