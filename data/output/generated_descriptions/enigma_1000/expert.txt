description: The Enigma 1000 problem is a classic puzzle where the goal is to find a solution to an equation represented by letters, each standing for a different digit, such that the equation is satisfied. The equation is ENIGMA = M * TIMES, where each letter corresponds to a unique digit, and no number starts with zero.

objective: satisfy - The objective of this problem is to find a solution that satisfies all the constraints, rather than minimizing or maximizing a specific value.
