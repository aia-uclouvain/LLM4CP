The Ormat Game
The Ormat game is about covering colored squares in a grid with transparent plastic overlays that have patterns of black dots. 
The main variables are the grid of colored squares, the overlays with black dots, and the number of overlays used. 
The basic restrictions are that the colored squares must be covered by the black dots, and the blank squares must not be covered. 
The goal is to use as few overlays as possible to cover all the colored squares.
