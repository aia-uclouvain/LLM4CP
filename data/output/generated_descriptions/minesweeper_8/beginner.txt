Minesweeper problem
The problem is about a game where you have to figure out where mines are hidden in a grid. 
The main variables are the grid cells, which can either have a mine or a number showing how many mines are nearby.
The basic restrictions are that each cell can only have one thing in it (either a mine or a number), and the numbers in the cells must be correct according to the rules of the game. 
The goal of the problem is to correctly fill in the entire grid, figuring out where all the mines are and what numbers should be in the other cells.
