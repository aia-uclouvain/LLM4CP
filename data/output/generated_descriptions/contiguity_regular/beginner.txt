The problem is called Global Contiguity. 
It's about arranging a series of 0s and 1s in a line so that all the 1s are together. 
The main variable is "arr", which is an array of 0s and 1s. 
The basic restriction is that all the 1s in the array must appear one after the other, without any 0s in between. 
The goal of the problem is to find an arrangement of 0s and 1s that satisfies this rule.
