The problem is called Max Index. 
It's about finding the position of the largest value in a list of numbers. 
The main variables are: variables (a list of numbers), max_ind (the position of the largest number), and max_val (the largest number itself). 
The basic restrictions are that the list of numbers must match a given list, and max_ind and max_val must be related to the largest number in the list. 
The goal is to find the position and value of the largest number in the list, given that the list must match a specific set of numbers.
