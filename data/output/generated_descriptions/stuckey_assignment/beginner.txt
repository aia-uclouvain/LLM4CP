Assignment Problem
The problem is about assigning workers to products in a way that maximizes profit. 
The main variables are workers and products, which represent the people doing the work and the things they are working on. There's also a variable that keeps track of whether a worker is assigned to a product or not, and another that calculates the total profit.
The basic restrictions are that each worker can only work on one product, and each product can only have one worker assigned to it.
The goal is to maximize the total profit by finding the best assignment of workers to products.
