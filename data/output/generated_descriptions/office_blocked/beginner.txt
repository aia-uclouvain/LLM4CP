Office Blocked problem
The problem is about figuring out which of six employees work in which offices in a two-floor building. 
The main variables are the names of the employees: Arthur, Bella, Craig, Duncan, Elizabeth, and Francesca, which represent the offices they occupy.
The basic restrictions are: Arthur's office is directly above Bella's, Bella's office is next to Duncan's, Elizabeth's office number is lower than Francesca's, and Craig's office is next to Francesca's.
The goal is to find out who works in which office, satisfying all the given restrictions.
