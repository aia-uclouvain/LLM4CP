The problem is called "min_n" or "minimum of rank n". 
It's about finding the smallest distinct value in a list of numbers that appears at a certain position when the list is sorted. 
The main variables are: 
- "variables" which is a list of numbers, 
- "t_min" which is the smallest distinct value we're looking for, 
- "rank" which is the position of "t_min" in the sorted list.
The basic restrictions are: 
- "t_min" must be one of the numbers in the list, 
- "rank" is the number of distinct values in the list that are smaller than "t_min".
The goal is to find the values of "variables", "t_min", and "rank" that satisfy these restrictions.
