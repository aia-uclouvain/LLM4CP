Appointment Scheduling Problem
The problem is about scheduling interviews between an employer and multiple people. 
The main variables are the time slots and the people to be interviewed, where each person has a schedule showing which time slots they are available for. 
The basic restrictions are that each person can only be assigned to one time slot, and each time slot can only be assigned to one person. Additionally, a person can only be assigned to a time slot if they are available at that time. 
The goal of the problem is to find a schedule that assigns each person to a time slot, if possible, while satisfying all the restrictions.
