The problem is called "Scheduling Speakers". 
It is about scheduling speakers for a conference in a way that minimizes the total time spent by all speakers. 
The main variables are the starting time and ending time for each speaker, and the availability of each speaker at different time slots. 
The restrictions are that each speaker can only speak at certain time slots, and that no two speakers can speak at the same time. 
The goal is to find a schedule that allows all speakers to speak without overlapping and minimizes the total time spent by all speakers.
