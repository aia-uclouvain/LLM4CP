The problem is called Optimizing a Constraint problem. 
It's about finding the best way to adjust some numbers so that they satisfy certain rules and are as small as possible. 
The main variables are a, b, and rhs, which represent a list of numbers, a target number, and a calculated number based on the list and target. 
The basic restrictions are that all numbers must be non-negative, and certain combinations of numbers must be less than or equal to the target, while others must be greater than or equal to the target plus one. Additionally, each number in the list must be less than or equal to the previous number. 
The goal is to minimize the target number or the sum of the numbers in the list, depending on the objective.
