The basic restrictions or rules of the problem are that any two sequences can only share one identical element in the same position, and the sequences must be ordered in a specific way to avoid duplicates.

The goal of the problem is to find the longest list of sequences that satisfy these rules, with the aim of maximizing the number of sequences. In this case, the problem is trying to find a list of at least 25 sequences that meet the conditions.
