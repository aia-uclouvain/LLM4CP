3 Coins Problem
The problem is about flipping coins to make them all show the same side, either heads or tails, in just three moves.
The main variables are the coins, which can be either heads (0) or tails (1), and the moves, which are the steps taken to flip the coins.
The basic restrictions are that only one coin can be flipped at a time, and the coins must all be either heads or tails after three moves.
The goal is to find a sequence of three moves that makes all the coins show the same side, either all heads or all tails.
