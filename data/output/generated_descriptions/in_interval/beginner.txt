The problem is called "in_interval". 
It's about making sure a certain value is within a specific range, like a number being between 1 and 10. 
The main variables are "v", "low", and "up", which mean the value to check, the lowest allowed number, and the highest allowed number, respectively. 
The basic rule is that "v" must be greater than or equal to "low" and less than or equal to "up". 
The goal is to find a solution where "v" is within the allowed range defined by "low" and "up", and in this case, to confirm that the given value of "v" (which is 3) is indeed within the given range (from 2 to 5).
