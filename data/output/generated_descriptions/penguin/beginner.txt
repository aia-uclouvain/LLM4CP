Penguin problem 
This problem is about figuring out which birds can fly, given some rules about birds and their abilities. 
The main variables are Tweety and Toto, which represent two birds, and "flies" which shows whether each bird can fly or not. 
The basic rules are that birds can usually fly, but some birds, like penguins, are abnormal and cannot fly, and that Tweety is a penguin and Toto is an eagle. 
The goal of the problem is to determine which of the two birds, Tweety or Toto, can fly, based on these rules.
