Quasigroup problem 
The problem is about finding a special kind of multiplication table where each row and column has unique numbers and follows certain rules. 
The main variables are the numbers in the multiplication table, which represent the result of "multiplying" two numbers together in a special way. 
The basic restrictions are that each row and column must have unique numbers, and the table must follow a specific rule that relates the numbers in different positions. 
The goal of the problem is to find a table that satisfies all these rules, which is known as a quasigroup.
