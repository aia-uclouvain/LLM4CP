The problem is called the Strictly Decreasing, Increasing, or Decreasing Sequence problem. 
It's about arranging a set of numbers in a specific order, either from smallest to largest, largest to smallest, or in a way that each number is less than or equal to the previous one. 
The main variables are the numbers in the sequence, which are represented by the array "x". 
The basic restrictions or rules are that the numbers must be arranged in one of these specific orders: strictly decreasing (each number is larger than the next), strictly increasing (each number is smaller than the next), or decreasing (each number is less than or equal to the next). 
The goal of the problem is to find a sequence of numbers that satisfies one of these orders, and in this case, it's trying to find a sequence that is strictly decreasing and matches a given example sequence (8, 4, 3, 1).
