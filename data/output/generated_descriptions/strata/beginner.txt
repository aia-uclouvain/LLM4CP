Strata puzzle
The Strata puzzle is a game where you have to layer colored ribbons to match a given pattern. Imagine you have a grid of squares, and you want to fill it with colors in a specific way. You can make moves by choosing a row or column and filling it with a certain color. The goal is to find the shortest sequence of moves that will transform an empty grid into the desired pattern.

The main variables are: 
- move_color: the color of the move
- move_row: the row where the move is made
- move_column: the column where the move is made
- num_moves: the total number of moves
- y: the state of the grid after each move

The basic restrictions are: 
- You can only make a move in one row or one column at a time
- You can't change the color of a square that is already filled with the correct color
- You have to fill the entire grid with the correct colors

The goal is to minimize the number of moves (num_moves) needed to transform the empty grid into the desired pattern.
