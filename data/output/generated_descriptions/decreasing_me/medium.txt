The name of the problem is the Decreasing Sequence problem. 
This problem is about finding a sequence of numbers that are in decreasing order, meaning each number is less than or equal to the previous one. 
The main decision variables are the elements of the array `x`, which represent the sequence of numbers to be found. 
The key constraints are that the sequence must be decreasing, meaning each element must be less than or equal to the previous one, and the sequence must match a given example sequence. 
The problem's objective is to find a sequence that satisfies these constraints, rather than minimizing or maximizing a specific value, as the goal is to demonstrate the use of the decreasing constraint in MiniZinc.
