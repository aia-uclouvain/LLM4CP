Team Formation Problem
The Team Formation Problem is about dividing a group of people into teams of equal size. 
The main decision variables are the assignments of each person to a team, represented by the array x, where x[i] denotes the team number assigned to person i. 
The key constraints are that each team must have exactly n members, and the order of elements within each team and the order of the teams themselves do not matter. 
The problem's objective is to find all possible ways to form the teams, and the model uses a satisfaction approach, where it aims to find all feasible solutions that satisfy the given constraints, rather than optimizing a specific objective function.
