Team Formation Problem
The problem is about dividing a group of people into teams of the same size. 
The main variables are the number of teams (m), the size of each team (n), and the assignment of each person to a team (x). 
The basic restrictions are that each team must have exactly n members, and the order of the teams and the order of the members within each team do not matter. 
The goal of the problem is to find all possible ways to divide the people into teams, with the number of ways being calculated as (nm)!/n!mm!.
