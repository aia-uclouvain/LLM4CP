Combinatorial Auction Problem
The problem is about an auction where people can bid on groups of items, not just individual items. 
The main variables are: 
- packages: the groups of items that people are bidding on
- bids: the amount of money that people are offering for each group of items
- x: whether a bid is accepted or not
- total: the total amount of money that the auctioneer gets from the accepted bids.
The basic restrictions are: 
- each item can only be sold once
- if a bid is accepted, all the items in that bid must be sold to the bidder.
The goal is to maximize the total amount of money that the auctioneer gets from the accepted bids, by choosing which bids to accept and which to reject.
