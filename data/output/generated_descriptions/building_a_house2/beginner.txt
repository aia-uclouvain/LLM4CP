The problem is called Scheduling or Building a House. 
It's about building a house as quickly as possible by doing different tasks like erecting walls, carpentry for the roof, and painting, in the right order. 
The main variables are the tasks, their duration (how many days each task takes), and when each task starts and ends. 
The basic restrictions are that some tasks must be finished before others can start, like you can't start painting until the walls are erected and the roof is done. 
The goal is to find the order and timing of the tasks that allows the house to be built in the shortest amount of time, which is called the makespan.
