name: Sangraal Problem
description: The problem is about rescuing knights from the Foul Fiend's castle before the sun sets and the Sangraal arrives. Each knight has a specific time to be freed and prepared, and the goal is to determine how many knights can be rescued within the given time limit of 20 minutes.
variables: $start_i, end_i, rescued_i, z$ where $i = 1, \ldots, 8$. These variables represent the start time of rescuing each knight, the end time of rescuing and preparing each knight, a binary variable indicating whether each knight is rescued, and the total number of rescued knights, respectively.
constraints: $\forall i \in \{1, \ldots, 8\}: end_i = start_i + free_i + prep_i \land (end_i \leq time\_limit \leftrightarrow rescued_i = 1) \land cumulative(start, free, [1], 1)$. These constraints ensure that the end time of rescuing and preparing each knight is calculated correctly, that a knight is only considered rescued if they are prepared before the time limit, and that the cumulative resource constraint is satisfied, meaning that the total time spent rescuing knights at any given time does not exceed the available time.
objective: Maximize $z$, the total number of rescued knights.
