name: Post Office Problem
description: The Post Office Problem is a classic problem in constraint programming that involves scheduling workers at a post office. The problem requires determining the number of workers to start on each day of the week to meet the daily demand while minimizing the total cost. The cost of scheduling workers varies depending on the day of the week, with higher costs for working on weekends and holidays.
variables: $x_i$, where $i \in \{0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6\}$, representing the number of workers starting on day $i$ (Monday = 0, Tuesday = 1, ..., Sunday = 6). These decision variables determine the schedule of workers for the week.
constraints: $\forall i \in \{0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6\}, \sum_{j \in \{0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6\} \setminus \{(i+5) \mod 7, (i+6) \mod 7\}} x_j \geq Need_i$, where $Need_i$ is the demand for day $i$. This constraint ensures that the number of workers scheduled for each day meets the daily demand, excluding workers who are not available due to the 5-day schedule.
objective: Minimize $z = \sum_{i \in \{0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6\}} x_i \cdot Cost_i$, where $Cost_i$ is the cost of scheduling workers on day $i$. The objective is to find a schedule that minimizes the total cost while meeting the daily demand.
