Period Constraint Problem
The problem is about finding a sequence of numbers that repeats itself after a certain period. 
The main variables are: 
- x, which is a list of numbers 
- per_len, which is the length of the period 
- the_period, which is the sequence of numbers that repeats itself.
The basic restrictions are that the sequence must repeat itself after the period, and the period must be less than or equal to half the length of the sequence. 
The goal is to find a sequence that satisfies these restrictions, and to identify the repeating period within the sequence.
