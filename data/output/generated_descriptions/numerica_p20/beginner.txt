Circle and Parabola Intersection problem. 
This problem is about finding the points where a circle and a parabola intersect. 
The main variables are x and y, which represent the coordinates of the points where the circle and parabola meet. 
The basic restrictions are that these points must be on both the circle and the parabola, meaning they must satisfy two equations: the equation of the circle (x^2 + y^2 = 1) and the equation of the parabola (x^2 = y). 
The goal of the problem is to find the values of x and y that satisfy both equations, which will give us the intersection points of the circle and the parabola.
