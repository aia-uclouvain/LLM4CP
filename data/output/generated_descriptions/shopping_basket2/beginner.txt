Shopping Basket Problem
The problem is about finding the cheapest way to buy a list of items from different shops, considering the prices of the items and the delivery costs of the shops.
The main variables are: 
- x: which shop to buy each item from
- total: the total cost of buying all the items
The basic restrictions or rules are: 
- each item can only be bought from one shop
- if the total cost of items from a shop is below a certain limit, a delivery cost is added
- the total cost is the sum of the costs of all items and the delivery costs of the shops
The goal is to minimize the total cost, i.e., to find the cheapest way to buy all the items.
