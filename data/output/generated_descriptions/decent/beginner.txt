Decentralization problem
The problem is about deciding where to locate different departments of a company in various cities to maximize benefits while minimizing communication costs.
The main variables are: 
- d (department in city): whether a department is located in a particular city
- g (department in city and department in city): whether two departments are in the same or different cities
- tcost (total cost): the total benefit of locating departments in cities minus the communication costs between them
The basic restrictions or rules are: 
- each department must be located in one city
- at most three departments can be in each city
- the communication costs between departments in different cities must be considered
The goal of the problem is to maximize the total benefit (tcost) by finding the best location for each department in a city, taking into account the benefits and communication costs.
