Layout Problem
The problem is about fitting different shapes into a grid, like a puzzle. 
The main variables are the grid, which is the space where the shapes will be placed, and the shapes themselves, which have different forms and sizes. 
The basic restrictions are that each shape must be placed entirely within the grid, and no part of a shape can overlap with another shape. 
The goal of the problem is to find a way to place all the shapes in the grid so that every cell in the grid is either empty or occupied by a shape, and each shape is placed correctly according to its form.
