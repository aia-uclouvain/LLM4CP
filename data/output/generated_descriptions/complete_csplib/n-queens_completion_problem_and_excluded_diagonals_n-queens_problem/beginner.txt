N-Queens Completion Problem
The problem is about finding a way to place queens on a chessboard such that no two queens attack each other, given some pre-placed queens and restrictions on certain diagonals.
The main variables are the positions of the queens on the chessboard, represented as a matrix or a set of coordinates.
The basic restrictions are that no two queens can be in the same row, column, or diagonal, and that certain diagonals are restricted.
The goal is to find a solution that satisfies all the restrictions and places the maximum number of queens on the board.
