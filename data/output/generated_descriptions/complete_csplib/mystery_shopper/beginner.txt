Mystery Shopper Problem
The problem is about assigning visitors to visitees in a way that meets certain rules, like making sure each visitor and visitee is unique each week, and that visitors and visitees are from different groups. 
The main variables are: 
- r: the visitor assigned to each visitee at each week
- e: the visitee assigned to each visitor at each week
- rg: the visitor group for each visitee at each week
- eg: the visitee group for each visitor at each week
The basic restrictions are: 
- each visitor and visitee must be unique each week
- each visitor and visitee must be from a different group
- the groups of visitors and visitees must be different for each visitor and visitee
The goal is to find an assignment of visitors to visitees that satisfies these rules, without a specific objective to minimize or maximize, the focus is on finding a valid assignment.
