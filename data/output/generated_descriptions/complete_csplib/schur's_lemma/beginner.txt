Schur's Lemma
The problem is about distributing balls into boxes in such a way that no box contains a specific combination of balls. 
The main variables are the number of balls (n) and the number of boxes (d or c), and the box where each ball is placed (x). 
The basic restrictions are that for any three balls where one is the sum of the other two, they cannot all be in the same box. 
The goal of the problem is to find a way to distribute the balls into the boxes that satisfies this restriction.
