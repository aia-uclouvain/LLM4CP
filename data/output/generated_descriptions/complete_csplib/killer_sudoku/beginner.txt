Killer Sudoku
Killer Sudoku is a puzzle that combines elements of Sudoku and Kakuro. The goal is to fill a 9x9 grid with numbers from 1 to 9, such that each row, column, and 3x3 sub-grid contains each number exactly once. Additionally, the sum of all numbers in a "cage" (a group of cells marked with a dashed line) must match the small number printed in the corner of the cage.
The main variables are the numbers in the 9x9 grid, which represent the possible values for each cell. The constraints are that each row, column, and 3x3 sub-grid must contain each number exactly once, and the sum of all numbers in a cage must match the small number printed in the corner of the cage.
