Auction Problem
The problem is about holding an auction where people bid on items, and the goal is to choose which bids to accept to get the most value.
The main variables are bids (which are offers to buy items) and x (which represents whether a bid is accepted or not). 
The basic restrictions are that if two bids include the same item, only one of them can be accepted.
The goal is to maximize the total value of the accepted bids.
