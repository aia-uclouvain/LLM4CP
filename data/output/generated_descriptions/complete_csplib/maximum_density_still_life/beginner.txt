Maximum Density Still Life
The problem is about finding the maximum number of cells that can be alive in a grid, following the rules of Conway's Game of Life. 
The main variables are the cells in the grid, which can be either alive (1) or dead (0). 
The basic restrictions are that all border cells are dead, alive cells have between 2 and 3 alive neighbors, and dead cells must not have 3 alive neighbors. 
The goal is to maximize the number of alive cells in the grid.
