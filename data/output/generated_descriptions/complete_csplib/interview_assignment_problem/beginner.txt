Student Interview Assignment Problem
The problem is about assigning students to companies for interviews, where each student has a preference for which companies they want to interview with. 
The main variables are: 
- Student preferences: a list of numbers showing how much each student likes each company
- Company capacities: the maximum number of students each company can interview
- Assignments: which company each student is assigned to for an interview
The basic restrictions are: 
- Each student can only be assigned to a certain number of companies
- Each company can only interview a certain number of students
- Each student must be assigned to different companies for each interview
The goal is to minimize the total "regret" of the students, which means assigning students to their preferred companies as much as possible, while also following the rules and restrictions.
