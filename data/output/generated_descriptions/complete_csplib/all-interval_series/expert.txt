name: All Interval Series Problem
description: The problem is to find a series of pitch classes where each class occurs exactly once and the intervals between neighboring notes cover the full set of intervals from the minor second to the major seventh.
variables: $x = (x_1, x_2, ..., x_n)$ and $d = (d_1, d_2, ..., d_{n-1})$, where $x_i$ represents the pitch class at position $i$ and $d_i$ represents the interval between $x_i$ and $x_{i+1}$.
constraints: $x$ is a permutation of $\{0, 1, ..., n-1\}$, $d$ is a permutation of $\{1, 2, ..., n-1\}$, and $d_i = |x_{i+1} - x_i|$ for $i = 1, 2, ..., n-1$.
objective: The problem is a satisfaction problem, where the goal is to find a series that satisfies all the constraints, rather than minimizing or maximizing a specific value.
