Maximum Clique Problem
The problem is about finding a group of friends in a social network where everyone in the group knows each other. 
The main variables are the nodes or people in the network, and a variable that says whether each person is in the group or not.
The basic rules are that if two people do not know each other, they cannot both be in the group. 
The goal is to find the largest possible group of friends where everyone knows each other.
