Nursing Workload Problem
The problem is about assigning nurses to patients in a hospital while ensuring that each nurse has a fair and manageable workload. 
The main variables are: 
- p, which represents the nurse assigned to each patient, 
- w, which represents the workload of each nurse. 
The basic restrictions are: 
- each nurse must be assigned to a minimum and maximum number of patients, 
- patients from the same zone cannot be assigned to the same nurse, 
- the workload of each nurse is calculated based on the demands of the patients assigned to them, 
- the workload of nurses must be in increasing order. 
The goal is to minimize the sum of the squares of the workloads of all nurses, which means finding a balance in the workload of each nurse to ensure fairness and efficiency.
