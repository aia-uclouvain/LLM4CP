Capacitated Vehicle Routing Problem
The problem is about delivering goods from a central location (called a depot) to several other locations using a fleet of vehicles, each with a limited capacity. 
The main variables are: 
- the locations where goods need to be delivered 
- the vehicles used for delivery 
- the routes each vehicle takes 
- the amount of goods each vehicle can carry.
The basic restrictions are: 
- each vehicle can only carry a certain amount of goods 
- each location must receive its goods 
- vehicles must start and end at the depot 
- each vehicle can only make one trip.
The goal is to find the most efficient way to deliver all the goods while minimizing the total distance traveled by all vehicles.
