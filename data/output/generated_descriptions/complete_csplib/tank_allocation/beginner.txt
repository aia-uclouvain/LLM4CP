Tank Allocation 
The problem is about filling tanks with different types of cargo in a way that follows certain rules. 
The main variables are the tanks, the types of cargo, and how the cargo is allocated to the tanks. In simple terms, the variables represent which tank holds which type of cargo, or if a tank is left empty. 
The basic restrictions are that each tank can only hold one type of cargo or be empty, certain types of cargo cannot be placed in certain tanks, and some types of cargo cannot be placed in neighboring tanks. 
The goal is to maximize the number of empty tanks while making sure that all the required cargo is allocated and the restrictions are followed.
