Smullyan's Portia problem
The problem is about finding a portrait in one of three caskets, Gold, Silver, or Lead, based on inscriptions on the caskets. 
The main variables are "portrait" and "inscriptions", where "portrait" means which casket the portrait is in, and "inscriptions" means what is written on each casket.
The basic restrictions are that only one casket can have the portrait, and the inscriptions on the caskets must be true or false based on the problem rules. 
The goal of the problem is to figure out which casket the portrait is in, based on the inscriptions.
