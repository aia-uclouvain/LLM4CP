Nim Game
The problem is about a game where two players take turns removing matchsticks from a pile. 
The main variables are the number of matchsticks taken by each player at each turn, who makes each move, and who wins the game. 
The basic restrictions are that each player must take one or two matchsticks at a turn, and the player who takes the last matchstick wins. 
The goal is to determine who wins the game when both players make the best possible moves.
