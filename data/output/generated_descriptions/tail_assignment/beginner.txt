Tail Assignment 
The problem is about assigning flights to aircraft in a way that meets certain rules and requirements. 
The main variables are: 
- n_flights: the total number of flights 
- n_planes: the total number of aircraft 
- maint_freq: how often an aircraft needs to visit a maintenance facility 
- route: the sequence of flights assigned to each aircraft 
The basic restrictions or rules are: 
- Each aircraft can only make flights that it is capable of making 
- Each flight can only be made once 
- Aircraft must visit a maintenance facility regularly 
- The sequence of flights for each aircraft must be valid and follow the rules of the flight network 
The goal of the problem is to find a valid assignment of flights to aircraft that meets all the rules and requirements, ensuring that every flight is made exactly once and every aircraft visits a maintenance facility frequently enough.
