The problem is called Fix Points. 
It's about finding a special kind of arrangement where some items are in their correct positions. 
The main variables are x, which is a list of items that can be in any position from 1 to n, and z, which is the number of items that are in their correct positions. 
The basic restrictions are that each item in the list x can only be in one position, and the number of items in their correct positions, z, must be exactly 4. 
The goal of the problem is to find an arrangement of items in the list x such that exactly 4 items are in their correct positions.
