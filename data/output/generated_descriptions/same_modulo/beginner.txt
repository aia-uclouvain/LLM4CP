Same Modulo Problem
The problem is about comparing two sets of numbers to see if they have the same pattern when divided by a certain number.
The main variables are v1 and v2, which are lists of numbers, and M, which is the number by which we divide the numbers in v1 and v2. 
The basic restrictions are that the numbers in v1 and v2 must be between 1 and 9, and M is 3. The problem also requires that the pattern of remainders when dividing the numbers in v1 by M is the same as the pattern of remainders when dividing the numbers in v2 by M.
The goal of the problem is to check if the given lists v1 and v2 satisfy the same_modulo constraint, meaning they have the same pattern of remainders when divided by M.
