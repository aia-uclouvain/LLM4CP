Appointment Scheduling Problem
The problem is about scheduling interviews between an employer and several people. 
The main variables are the people and the time slots available for the interviews, where each person has a schedule showing which time slots they are free. 
The basic restrictions are that each person can only be assigned to a time slot when they are free, and each time slot can only be assigned to one person. 
The goal of the problem is to find a schedule that assigns each person to a time slot, if possible, and to indicate if it's impossible to find such a schedule.
