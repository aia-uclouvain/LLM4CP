Kakuro puzzle
The problem is about filling in numbers in a puzzle so that they add up to certain totals and no number is repeated in any row or column. 
The main variables are the numbers that go in each empty space, which can be any number from 1 to 9.
The basic restrictions are that the numbers in each group must add up to a certain total, and no number can be used more than once in any group.
The goal is to find a solution where all the numbers are filled in correctly, following the rules of the puzzle.
