Local Art Theft Problem
The problem is about figuring out who committed a crime after interviewing six suspects. 
The main variables are the names of the suspects: Alan, Brian, Charlie, Dave, Eddie, and Freddie, which represent whether each person is telling the truth or not.
The basic restrictions are that each suspect makes statements about who did not commit the crime, and exactly four of the suspects are lying once. 
The goal is to find out who committed the crime by analyzing the statements and lies.
