The problem is called "alldifferent_on_intersection". 
It's about making sure that when two lists of numbers have some numbers in common, each of those common numbers appears only once in each list. 
The main variables are "x" and "y", which are lists of numbers. 
The basic restrictions are that "x" must be equal to a specific list of numbers [5,9,1,5], and "y" must be equal to another specific list of numbers [2,1,6,9,6,2]. Also, the numbers that appear in both "x" and "y" must appear only once in each list. 
The goal of the problem is to find values for "x" and "y" that satisfy these restrictions.
