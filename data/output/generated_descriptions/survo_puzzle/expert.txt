name: Survo Puzzle
description: The Survo puzzle is a logic puzzle where the task is to fill an m * n table with integers 1,2,...,m*n such that each of these numbers appears only once and their row and column sums are equal to integers given on the bottom and the right side of the table.
variables: $x_{i,j}$, where $i \in \{1,2,...,r\}$ and $j \in \{1,2,...,c\}$. These are decision variables representing the value in the $i^{th}$ row and $j^{th}$ column of the table.
constraints: 
$\bigwedge_{i=1}^{r} \bigwedge_{j=1}^{c} x_{i,j} \in \{1,2,...,r*c\}$, 
$\bigwedge_{i=1}^{r} \bigwedge_{j=1}^{c} x_{i,j} = matrix_{i,j}$ if $matrix_{i,j} > 0$, 
$\bigwedge_{i=1}^{r} \sum_{j=1}^{c} x_{i,j} = rowsums_{i}$, 
$\bigwedge_{j=1}^{c} \sum_{i=1}^{r} x_{i,j} = colsums_{j}$, 
$\bigwedge_{i=1}^{r} \bigwedge_{j=1}^{c} \bigwedge_{k=1}^{r} \bigwedge_{l=1}^{c} (i \neq k \vee j \neq l) \rightarrow x_{i,j} \neq x_{k,l}$. 
These constraints ensure that each cell in the table contains a unique integer between 1 and $r*c$, the given values in the table are respected, the sum of each row and column equals the given row and column sums, and all numbers in the table are unique.
objective: The objective of the Survo puzzle is to find a solution that satisfies all the constraints, there is no explicit objective function to minimize or maximize. The goal is to find an assignment of values to the decision variables $x_{i,j}$ such that all constraints are satisfied.
