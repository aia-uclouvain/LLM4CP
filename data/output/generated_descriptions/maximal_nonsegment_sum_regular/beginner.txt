The problem is called Maximal Non-Segment Sum. 
It's about finding the largest possible sum of numbers from a given list, with a condition: the numbers you choose cannot be next to each other in the list. 
The main variables are the list of numbers (s) and a set of choices (x) that say whether each number should be included in the sum or not. 
The basic rule is that you cannot choose numbers that are next to each other in the list. 
The goal is to find the largest possible sum of the chosen numbers.
