Bit Vector Problem
The problem is about finding a number that Alice picked, where she multiplies it by two and then inverts the last 4 bits of the result, and the result is the same as the initial number.
The main variables are: 
- num: the initial number Alice picked
- num_x: the binary representation of num
- num2: the result of multiplying num by 2
- num2_x: the binary representation of num2
- num3: the result of inverting the last 4 bits of num2
- num3_x: the binary representation of num3
The basic restrictions are: 
- num is multiplied by 2 to get num2
- the last 4 bits of num2 are inverted to get num3
- num is equal to num3
The goal is to find the value of num that satisfies these conditions.
