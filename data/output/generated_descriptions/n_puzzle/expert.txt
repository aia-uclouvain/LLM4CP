name: 15-Puzzle (n-puzzle) Problem
description: The 15-puzzle problem is a classic problem in the field of artificial intelligence and computer science. It consists of a 4x4 grid of numbered tiles, with one empty space (represented by the number 16). The goal is to rearrange the tiles so that they are in ascending order, with the empty space at the end. The puzzle can be solved by sliding adjacent tiles into the empty space.
variables: $x_{i,j}, moves_{i,j}, operations_i, check_i, check\_ix, changes_i, puzzle_j, valid\_moves_{i,j}, num\_sols$ 
These variables represent the following: $x_{i,j}$ is the value of the tile at position $j$ at step $i$, $moves_{i,j}$ represents the move made at step $i$, $operations_i$ is the type of operation (0 for no move, 1 for a move) made at step $i$, $check_i$ is a flag indicating whether the puzzle is solved at step $i$, $check\_ix$ is the index of the first step where the puzzle is solved, $changes_i$ is the number of changes made at step $i$, $puzzle_j$ is the initial value of the tile at position $j$, $valid\_moves_{i,j}$ is a matrix indicating the valid moves for each tile, and $num\_sols$ is the number of steps to solve the puzzle.
constraints: $\forall j \in 1..t: x_{1,j} = puzzle_j$, $\forall i \in 2..num\_sols: changes_i = \sum_{j=1}^t (x_{i,j} \neq x_{i-1,j})$, $\forall i \in 2..num\_sols: (changes_i = 2 \lor changes_i = 0)$, $\forall i \in 2..num\_sols: (check_i = 1 \leftrightarrow \forall j \in 1..t: x_{i,j} = j)$, $\forall i \in 2..num\_sols: (same(x_{i-1}, x_i) \lor (moves_{i-1,2} = moves_i \land x_{i-1,moves_i} = t \land valid\_moves_{moves_i,1}, moves_i = 1))$ 
These constraints ensure the following: the initial state of the puzzle is set, the number of changes at each step is either 0 or 2, the puzzle is solved when all tiles are in ascending order, and each move is either a no-op or a valid move.
objective: minimize $check\_ix$ 
The objective is to find the shortest sequence of moves that solves the puzzle, which is achieved by minimizing the index of the first step where the puzzle is solved.
