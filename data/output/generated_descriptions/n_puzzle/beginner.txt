15-Puzzle Problem
The 15-puzzle problem is about rearranging a set of numbered tiles in a 4x4 grid to be in order from 1 to 15, with the blank space (number 16) at the bottom right corner.
The main variables are: 
- puzzle: the initial configuration of the tiles
- moves: the sequence of moves to solve the puzzle
- x: the state of the puzzle at each step
- operations: the type of move (either "same" or "move") at each step
The basic restrictions are: 
- the tiles can only move up, down, left, or right into the blank space
- only one tile can move at a time
- the puzzle starts in a given configuration and ends in a solved state
The goal is to find the shortest sequence of moves that solves the puzzle, which is achieved by minimizing the number of steps (check_ix) needed to reach the solved state.
