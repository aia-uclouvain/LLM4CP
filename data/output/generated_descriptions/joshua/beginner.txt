Joshua and his rats puzzle
The problem is about finding the minimum total number of days it takes for Joshua to weigh his 9 rats, with certain rules to follow. 
The main variables are: 
- x, which represents the day each rat is weighed, 
- d, which is a helper variable to ensure the rules are followed, 
- tot, which is the total number of days it takes to weigh all the rats.
The basic restrictions are: 
- each rat must be weighed on a different day, 
- the day a rat is weighed must be at least one day after the previous rat, 
- there's a specific rule about the difference in days between the weighings of three rats that must be followed.
The goal of the problem is to minimize the total number of days, tot, it takes to weigh all the rats.
