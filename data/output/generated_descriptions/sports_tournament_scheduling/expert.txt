name: Round Robin Tournament Scheduling
description: The problem involves scheduling a round robin tournament for a given number of teams, where each team plays every other team exactly once, and the schedule is divided into weeks and periods. The goal is to find a schedule that satisfies the constraints of the tournament, including ensuring that each team plays every other team, and that the schedule is balanced and fair.
variables: $m_{w,p}, x_{w,p}, y_{w,p}, sched(w, p, ts)$, these are decision variables that represent the match at week $w$ and period $p$, the first team and the second team for the match at week $w$ and period $p$, and the schedule of teams for each week, period, and set of teams, respectively. 
constraints: $\forall t \in Team, \forall w \in Week, \exists (p, ts) \in sched(w, _, _) : t \in ts$, $\forall t \in Team, \forall p \in Period, \sum_{w, ts} (t \in ts) \leq 2$, $|sched(_, _, \{t1, t2\})| = 1$, these constraints ensure that each team plays every other team, that each team plays at most two times in each period, and that each pair of teams plays each other exactly once, respectively.
objective: The objective of the problem is to find a feasible schedule that satisfies all the constraints, there is no explicit objective function to minimize or maximize.
