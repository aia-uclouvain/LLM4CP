Differ From At Least K Positions problem.
This problem is about comparing two lists of numbers to see how many places they are different. 
The main variables are two lists of numbers, a1 and a2, and a number k that represents how many differences we are looking for.
The basic restrictions are that the two lists must be the same length, and we want to find out if they differ in at least k positions.
The goal of the problem is to check if the two lists differ in at least k positions, and to find the values of the lists and k that make this true.
