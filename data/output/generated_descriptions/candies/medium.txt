The name of the problem is the Candies problem. 
This problem is about a teacher, Alice, who wants to distribute candies to her students based on their performance ratings while minimizing the total number of candies given. 
The main decision variables are the array `x`, where `x[i]` represents the number of candies given to the `i-th` student. 
The key constraints are that each student must receive at least one candy, and if a student has a higher rating than their neighbor, they must receive more candies than that neighbor. 
The problem's objective is to minimize the total number of candies given, which is represented by the variable `z`, the sum of all elements in the `x` array.
