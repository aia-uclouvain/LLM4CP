Team Picking Problem
The problem is about dividing a group of people into two teams in a fair way, considering each person's strength or ability. 
The main variables are the number of people (n), each person's strength (s), the team each person belongs to (x), and the difference in strength between the two teams (d). 
The basic restrictions are that each team must have the same number of people, and the first person is assigned to team 1. 
The goal is to minimize the difference in strength between the two teams, making them as balanced as possible.
