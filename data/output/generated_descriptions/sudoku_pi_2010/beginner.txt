Pi Day Sudoku 2010
This problem is about solving a Sudoku puzzle, which is a game where you have to fill in numbers on a grid. 
The main variables are the numbers that go in each square of the grid, which are represented by the variable x. 
The basic restrictions or rules of the problem are that each row, column, and small square (3x3) can only have each number once, and some numbers are already given. 
The goal of the problem is to fill in all the numbers so that the rules are satisfied, which means finding a solution that works for the entire grid.
