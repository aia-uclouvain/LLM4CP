Eye Drop Optimization Problem
The problem is about finding the best way to take eye drops after a cataract operation. 
The main variables are the times when each type of eye drop should be taken, and the goal is to spread out these times as much as possible.
The basic restrictions are that there are a limited number of hours in the day, each type of drop can only be taken a certain number of times, and there can only be one drop per hour. 
The goal is to maximize the dispersion of the drop times, which means spreading them out as much as possible.
