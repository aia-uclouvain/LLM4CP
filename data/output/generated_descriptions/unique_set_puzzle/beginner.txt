The problem is called the "Unique Set Representing Number Puzzle" or "Bilateral Projects" problem. 
It's about choosing the smallest group of people that can represent all the given teams, with each team having two members and each person being part of one or more teams. 
The main variables are the teams, represented by pairs of numbers, and the people, also represented by numbers. 
The basic restrictions are that only one person from each team must be chosen, and the same person can be part of multiple teams. 
The goal is to minimize the number of people chosen, so that all teams are still represented.
