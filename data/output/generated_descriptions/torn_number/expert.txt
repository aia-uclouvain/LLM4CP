name: Torn Number Puzzle
description: The Torn Number Puzzle is a mathematical puzzle where a four-digit number is torn in half, and the two halves are added together and squared to produce the original number. The goal is to find another four-digit number with all different digits that satisfies this property.
variables: $D_0, D_1, D_2, D_3, Sum$ - These variables represent the digits of the four-digit number ($D_0, D_1, D_2, D_3$) and the sum of the two halves ($Sum$), where $D_0$ and $D_1$ are the digits of the first half, and $D_2$ and $D_3$ are the digits of the second half.
constraints: $D_3 \neq 0, Sum = D_3 \cdot 10 + D_2 + D_1 \cdot 10 + D_0, Sum \cdot Sum = D_3 \cdot 1000 + D_2 \cdot 100 + D_1 \cdot 10 + D_0, D_3 \cdot 10 + D_2 \neq 30, D_1 \cdot 10 + D_0 \neq 30, D_3 \cdot 10 + D_2 \neq 25, D_1 \cdot 10 + D_0 \neq 25, all\_different([D_0, D_1, D_2, D_3])$ - These constraints ensure that the second half of the number is not zero, the sum of the two halves is calculated correctly, the squared sum equals the original number, the two halves are not equal to 30 or 25, and all digits are different.
objective: satisfy - The objective of this problem is to find a solution that satisfies all the given constraints, rather than minimizing or maximizing a specific value.
