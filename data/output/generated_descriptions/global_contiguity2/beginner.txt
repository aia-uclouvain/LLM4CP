The problem is called Global Contiguity. 
It's about arranging a series of 0s and 1s in a line so that all the 1s are together. 
The main variable is "x", which is an array of 0s and 1s. 
The basic restrictions are that each position in the array can only be 0 or 1, and all the 1s must be next to each other, not spread out. 
The goal is to find an arrangement of 0s and 1s that satisfies this rule, without trying to minimize or maximize anything, just to find a valid solution.
