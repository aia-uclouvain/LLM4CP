The problem is called the Graceful Labeling problem. 
It's about labeling points (called nodes) in a special kind of map (called a tree) with numbers, so that the differences between the numbers on connected points are all unique. 
The main variables are the labels, which are the numbers given to each node. 
The basic restrictions are that each node must have a unique label, and the absolute difference between the labels of two connected nodes must be unique for each connection. 
The goal of the problem is to find a set of labels that satisfy these restrictions.
