The Interns problem
The problem is about finding the day of the week when all three hospital interns are on call at the same time. 
The main variables are the interns' schedules, which are represented by a table showing whether each intern is on call (1) or off (0) for each day of the week. 
The basic restrictions are: all three interns are on call together only once a week, no intern works three days in a row, and no two interns are off on the same day more than once a week. Additionally, each intern has specific days off. 
The goal is to find the day of the week when all three interns are on call, given their individual schedules and the restrictions.
