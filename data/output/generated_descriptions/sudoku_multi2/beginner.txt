Multi Sudoku
The problem is about solving two connected Sudoku puzzles at the same time. 
The main variables are s1 and s2, which represent the two Sudoku boards. 
The basic restrictions are that each row, column, and 3x3 box in both boards must contain the numbers 1-9 without repeating any number, and the overlapping parts of the two boards must be the same. 
The goal is to find a solution that satisfies these rules for both boards.
