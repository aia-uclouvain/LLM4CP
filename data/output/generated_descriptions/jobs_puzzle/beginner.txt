The name of the problem is the Jobs Puzzle. 
This problem is about figuring out which jobs are held by four people: Roberta, Thelma, Steve, and Pete. 
The main variables are the people (Roberta, Thelma, Steve, and Pete) and the eight different jobs they hold (chef, guard, nurse, clerk, police officer, teacher, actor, and boxer). 
The basic restrictions or rules of the problem are: each person holds exactly two jobs, the nurse is a male, the husband of the chef is the clerk, Roberta is not a boxer, and Pete has limited education. 
The goal of the problem is to determine which person holds which two jobs, given the restrictions and rules provided.
