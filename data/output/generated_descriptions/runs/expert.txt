name: Longest Run 
description: The problem is to find a sequence of numbers that satisfies certain constraints and determine the number of runs in the sequence. A run is a sequence of identical numbers. 
variables: $x = (x_1, x_2, ..., x_n)$, $r$: $x$ is an array of decision variables representing the sequence of numbers, where each $x_i$ can take a value from 1 to $n$, and $r$ is a decision variable representing the number of runs in the sequence.
constraints: $cp1d(x, [1,1,2,2,1,1])$, $runs(r, x)$: The first constraint ensures that the sequence $x$ is equal to the given sequence $[1,1,2,2,1,1]$. The second constraint calculates the number of runs in the sequence $x$ and assigns it to $r$.
objective: satisfy: The objective of the problem is to find a sequence that satisfies the given constraints, rather than minimizing or maximizing a specific value.
