Cookie Monster Problem
The problem is about a monster who wants to eat all the cookies from several jars in the fewest number of moves. 
The main variables are the number of cookies in each jar and the number of cookies the monster eats from each jar in each move. 
The basic restrictions are that the monster must eat the same number of cookies from each jar in each move, and he cannot eat more cookies from a jar than it contains. 
The goal is to find the smallest number of moves the monster needs to empty all the jars.
