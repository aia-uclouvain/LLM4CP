The problem is called "Choose your crew". 
It's about hiring the right crew members for a sea voyage, where each member has different skills in fishing, sailing, and navigation, and demands a certain salary. 
The main variables are the crew members, their skills in fishing, sailing, and navigation, and their salaries. 
In simple terms, the crew members are like workers, and their skills are like how good they are at certain jobs. 
The basic restrictions are that the total skills of the chosen crew members must be at least 15 in each of the three skill categories. 
The goal is to minimize the total cost of the crew, which means finding the cheapest combination of crew members that still meets the required skill levels.
