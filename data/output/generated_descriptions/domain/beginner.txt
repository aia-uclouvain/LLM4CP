Domain Problem
The problem is about making sure a group of numbers are within a certain range.
The main variables are x, low, and up. x is a list of numbers, low is the smallest allowed number, and up is the largest allowed number.
The basic rules are that all numbers in the list x must be greater than or equal to low and less than or equal to up.
The goal is to find values for x, low, and up that satisfy these rules, and then show what these values are.
