Golf Course Generation
The problem is about creating a random 18-hole golf course with specific rules. 
The main variables are the hole lengths, which can be 3, 4, or 5. 
The basic restrictions are that there must be 18 holes, each hole must be 3, 4, or 5 units long, and the total length of all holes must add up to 72 units. 
The goal is to generate all possible combinations of hole lengths that meet these rules, with each combination having some chance of being selected.
