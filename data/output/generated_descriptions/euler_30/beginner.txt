Euler problem 30 
This problem is about finding special numbers that can be expressed as the sum of fifth powers of their digits. 
The main variables are the digits of these special numbers and the numbers themselves. 
The basic restrictions are that each digit must be between 0 and 9, and the number must be greater than 1 and can be expressed as the sum of fifth powers of its digits. 
The goal is to find the sum of all these special numbers.
