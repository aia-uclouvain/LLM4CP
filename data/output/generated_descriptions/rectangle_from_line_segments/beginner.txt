Largest Possible Rectangle Problem
The problem is about building the largest possible rectangle using a set of given line segments. 
The main variables are the line segments of given lengths, the sides of the rectangle, and the area of the rectangle. 
The line segments can be combined to form the sides of the rectangle, but they cannot be broken or split. 
The basic restrictions are that the opposite sides of the rectangle must be of the same length, and all the line segments must be used. 
The goal is to maximize the area of the rectangle that can be built using all the given line segments.
