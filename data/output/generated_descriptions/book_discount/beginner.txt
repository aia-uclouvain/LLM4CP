Nice Discount Puzzle
The problem is about buying books over several days and getting a discount based on the total cost of books bought on previous days.
The main variables are: 
- x, which shows whether a specific book is bought on a specific day (1 means yes, 0 means no)
- t, which is the total cost of books bought on each day
- totcost, which is the total cost of all books after applying the discount
The basic restrictions are: 
- Each book can only be bought once
- The total cost of books bought on each day is calculated by adding the costs of all books bought that day
- A small discount is applied to the total cost based on the cost of books bought on previous days
The goal is to minimize the total cost of all books (totcost) by deciding which day to buy each book.
