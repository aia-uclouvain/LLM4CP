Max Activity Problem
The problem is about assigning kids to activities they prefer, with each activity having a limited number of spots. 
The main variables are: 
- x: which activity each kid is assigned to
- scores: how happy each kid is with their assigned activity, based on their preferences
- total_score: the total happiness of all kids
The basic restrictions are: 
- each kid can only be assigned to one activity
- each activity can only have a certain number of kids
- kids prefer some activities over others, and their happiness is based on how well their assignment matches their preferences
The goal is to maximize the total happiness of all kids, by assigning them to activities that match their preferences as closely as possible, without exceeding the limited number of spots in each activity.
