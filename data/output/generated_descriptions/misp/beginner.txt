Maximum Independent Set Problem
The problem is about finding the largest group of people who don't know each other in a big network of friends. 
The main variables are the number of people (n), the connections between people (E), and whether a person is in the group (x). 
The basic restrictions are that if two people know each other, they can't both be in the group. 
The goal is to find the largest group of people who don't know each other, which means maximizing the number of people in the group (obj).
