Babysitting puzzle
The problem is about figuring out the full names and ages of five children that Bonnie takes care of. 
The main variables are the first names (Keith, Libby, Margo, Nora, Otto) and last names (Fell, Gant, Hall, Ivey, Jule) of the children, and their ages (which range from 2 to 6 years old).
The basic restrictions are: each child has a unique first name, last name, and age, and there are specific relationships between the children's ages, such as one child being a certain number of years older than another.
The goal is to determine each child's full name and age, by satisfying all the given conditions and relationships between the children's ages and names.
