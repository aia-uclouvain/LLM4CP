Crossword Puzzle Problem
The problem is about filling in a crossword puzzle with words. 
The main variables are the letters to be filled in, represented by L, which can take values from 1 to 26, corresponding to the 26 letters of the alphabet.
The basic restrictions or rules of the problem are that certain combinations of letters must form valid words, and no two words can be identical. 
The goal of the problem is to find a solution that satisfies all the constraints, filling in the crossword puzzle with valid words.
