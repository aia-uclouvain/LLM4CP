Number Partitioning Problem
The problem is about finding all combinations of five numbers that add up to 100, with the condition that each number must be divisible by either 2 or 5.
The main decision variables are the array `x` of five variables, each representing one of the five numbers in the combination, and taking values between 1 and 100.
The key constraints are: the sum of the five numbers must equal 100, each number must be divisible by either 2 or 5, and the numbers must be in increasing order to avoid duplicate combinations.
The problem's objective is to find all valid combinations of five numbers that satisfy the given constraints, rather than minimizing or maximizing a specific value.
