Number Puzzle
The problem is about finding all possible combinations of five numbers that add up to 100, with a special condition: each number must be either even (divisible by 2) or end in 0 or 5 (divisible by 5).
The main variables are the five numbers, represented by x[1], x[2], x[3], x[4], and x[5], which are the numbers we're trying to find.
The basic restrictions are that the sum of these five numbers must be exactly 100, and each number must meet the condition of being even or ending in 0 or 5. Additionally, the numbers must be in increasing order to avoid duplicate combinations.
The goal of the problem is to find all combinations of five numbers that meet these conditions, rather than minimizing or maximizing a specific value.
