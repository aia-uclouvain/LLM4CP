Car Sequencing Problem
The problem is about sequencing cars on a production line, where each car has certain options that need to be installed. 
The main variables are the sequence of cars, the options installed in each car, and the number of cars of each class. 
The basic restrictions or rules of the problem are that each car must have the correct options installed, and the number of cars of each class must meet the demand. 
Additionally, there are constraints on the frequency of certain options, which cannot be installed too frequently in a row. 
The goal of the problem is to find a sequence of cars that meets all the constraints and demands.
