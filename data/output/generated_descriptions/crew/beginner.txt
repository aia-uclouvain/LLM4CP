Crew Allocation Problem
The problem is about assigning flight attendants to flights. 
The main variables are the crew members (20 people) and the flights (10 flights), and what they mean is which crew member is assigned to which flight.
The basic restrictions or rules of the problem are that each flight needs a certain number of crew members with specific skills, such as speaking certain languages, and each crew member needs to have two flights off after working one flight.
The goal of the problem is to minimize the number of crew members working, while still meeting all the requirements for each flight.
