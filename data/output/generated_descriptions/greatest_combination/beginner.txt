Greatest Combination Problem
The problem is about finding the best path through a grid of numbers, starting from the top and moving down, to get the highest total score. 
The main variables are the grid, which is a table of numbers, and the path, which is a list of positions in the grid that we choose to visit. 
The basic restrictions are that we can only move down, and we can only move one position to the left or right, or stay in the same position. 
The goal is to find the path that gives us the highest total score, by adding up the numbers in the positions we visit.
