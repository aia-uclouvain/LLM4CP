Work-shift scheduling problem
The problem is about assigning six workers to three working shifts while satisfying certain constraints on the number of workers per shift and individual worker availability.
The main decision variables are `x[1..p]`, where `x[i]` represents the shift assigned to worker `i`. The shifts are numbered from 1 to 3.
The key constraints are: 
- Each shift has a specific lower and upper bound on the number of workers that can be assigned to it. 
- Alan cannot work on the first shift. 
- Bob can only work on the third shift.
The problem's objective is to find a feasible assignment of workers to shifts that satisfies all the given constraints. There is no explicit objective function to minimize or maximize, the goal is to find a valid schedule.
