Finding Celebrities Problem
The problem is about identifying celebrities at a party, where a celebrity is a person that everybody at the party knows, but only knows other celebrities.

The main decision variable is `celebrities`, a set of people who are celebrities. The `party` array represents the graph of who knows who, where each index `i` corresponds to a person and the value at that index is a set of people that person `i` knows.

The key constraints are: 
- There is at least one celebrity at the party.
- Every person at the party knows all the celebrities.
- Each celebrity only knows other celebrities.

The objective of the problem is to find the set of people who are celebrities, satisfying the given constraints. The model does not explicitly minimize or maximize any variable, but rather finds a feasible solution that satisfies all the constraints, which in this case is the set of celebrities at the party.
