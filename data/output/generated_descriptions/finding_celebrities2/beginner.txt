Finding Celebrities Problem
The problem is about finding famous people, called "celebrities", at a party. 
The main variables are: 
- the number of people at the party (n), 
- who each person knows at the party (party), and 
- the group of celebrities (celebrities).
The basic restrictions are: 
- everyone at the party knows the celebrities, and 
- the celebrities only know other celebrities.
The goal is to find the group of people who are celebrities, meaning they are known by everyone and they only know other celebrities.
