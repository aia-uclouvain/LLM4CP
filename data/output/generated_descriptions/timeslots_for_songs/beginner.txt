Timeslot problem
The problem is about scheduling songs and a news segment into seven consecutive time slots for a broadcast. 
The main variables are the time slots for each song (G, H, L, O, P, S) and the news segment (News), which represent when each item will be played.
The basic restrictions are that L must be played right before O, the news segment must be played after L, and there must be exactly two time slots between G and P. 
The goal of the problem is to find all possible schedules that satisfy these restrictions, with the aim of generating a list of permutations that meet the conditions.
