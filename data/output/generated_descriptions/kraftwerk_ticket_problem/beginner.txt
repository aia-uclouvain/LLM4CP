Kraftwerk Concert Ticket Problem
The problem is about a group of friends who want to buy tickets to Kraftwerk concerts. Each person has a limited budget and can only buy tickets for one concert, with a maximum of 6 tickets. The friends have different preferences for the concerts, and the goal is to buy tickets in a way that maximizes the total happiness of the group.
The main variables are: 
- buy_concert: the concert that each person buys tickets for
- buy_num_tickets: the number of tickets each person buys
- attending: whether each person attends each concert
The basic restrictions are: 
- each person can only buy tickets for one concert
- each person can buy a maximum of 6 tickets
- the total number of tickets bought must equal the total number of concerts attended
- each person's budget limits the number of concerts they can attend
The goal is to maximize the total happiness of the group, which is calculated based on the preferences of each person for each concert.
