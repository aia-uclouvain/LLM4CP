The problem is called the Minimum Subsequence Sum problem. 
It's about finding a part of a list of numbers that adds up to the smallest possible total, where the numbers in the part must be in the same order as they appear in the original list. 
The main variables are: 
- x, which is the original list of numbers, 
- x_s, which is the part of the list we're trying to find, 
- ss_sum, which is the total of the numbers in x_s. 
The basic rules are: 
- x_s must be a part of x, 
- the numbers in x_s must be in the same order as they appear in x, 
- x_s must have a specific length (in this case, 4 numbers). 
The goal is to minimize ss_sum, which means finding the part of the list with the smallest possible total.
