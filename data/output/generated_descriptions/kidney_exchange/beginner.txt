Kidney Exchange Problem
The problem is about matching people who need a kidney transplant with people who are willing to donate a kidney, in a way that maximizes the number of successful transplants.
The main variables are: 
- num_people: the total number of people involved in the exchange
- compatible: a list of sets, where each set contains the numbers of people who can receive a kidney from a particular person
- x: a list of variables that represent who gives a kidney to whom
- z: the total number of successful transplants
The basic restrictions or rules are: 
- a person can only give a kidney to someone who is compatible with them
- if a person gives a kidney, they must also receive a kidney from someone else
- each person can only give one kidney and receive one kidney
The goal of the problem is to maximize the number of successful transplants, which is represented by the variable z.
