Farmer and Cow Problem
The problem is about a farmer who has 81 cows that produce different amounts of milk, and he wants to distribute these cows among his 9 sons. 
The main variables are the amount of milk each son gets and which cows are assigned to each son. 
The basic restrictions are that each son should get the same amount of milk, each son should get 9 cows, and all cows should be distributed. 
The goal is to find a way to distribute the cows so that each son gets the same amount of milk.
