Team Formation Problem
The problem is about dividing a group of people into teams of the same size. 
The main variables are the number of teams (m), the size of each team (n), and the people in each team (x). 
The basic restrictions are that each team must have the same number of people, and no person can be in more than one team. 
The goal is to find all possible ways to divide the people into teams, without considering the order of people within a team or the order of teams.
