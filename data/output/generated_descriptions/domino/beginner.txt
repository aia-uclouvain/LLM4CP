Domino Problem
The problem is about placing domino pieces on a board so that the numbers on the dominoes match the numbers on the board. 
The main variables are the board, which is a grid of numbers, the domino pieces, which are pairs of numbers, and the placement of the dominoes on the board.
The basic restrictions are that each domino piece must be used exactly twice, and the numbers on the dominoes must match the numbers on the board where they are placed. 
The goal of the problem is to find a way to place all the domino pieces on the board so that all the numbers match, and each domino piece is used exactly twice.
