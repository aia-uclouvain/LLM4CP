Scheduling Speakers
The problem is about scheduling six speakers to give talks in six time slots. 
The main variables are the speakers and the time slots they are assigned to, which means deciding which speaker talks at which time.
The basic restrictions are that each speaker can only talk once, and each speaker can only talk during certain time slots that they are available. 
The goal of the problem is to find a schedule where all speakers are assigned a time slot that they are available for, and no two speakers talk at the same time.
