The problem is called the Scheduling Speakers problem. 
It is about scheduling a set of speakers into a set of time slots, where each speaker has a specific set of available slots in which they can speak. 
The main decision variables are the array `x`, where `x[i]` represents the time slot assigned to speaker `i`. 
The key constraints are that each speaker must be assigned to one of their available slots, and no two speakers can be assigned to the same slot. 
The problem's objective is to find a feasible schedule that satisfies these constraints, meaning that every speaker is assigned a slot and no slot is assigned to more than one speaker.
