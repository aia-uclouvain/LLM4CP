Rubik's Cube Problem
The problem is about solving a Rubik's Cube, which is a 3D puzzle where you have to rotate the pieces to make each face a solid color. 
The main variables are the 48 pieces of the cube, the 6 basic moves (rotating a face clockwise or counter-clockwise), and the number of moves to solve the cube.
The basic restrictions are that each move can only rotate one face of the cube, and the goal is to make each face a solid color. 
The goal of the problem is to find the minimum number of moves to solve the cube, given a specific initial configuration.
