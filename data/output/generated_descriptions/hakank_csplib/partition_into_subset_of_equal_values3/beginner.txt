Partition Problem
The problem is about dividing a set of numbers into two or more subsets, where the sum of the numbers in each subset is equal.
The main variables are:
- `s`: the set of numbers to be divided
- `x`: an array that determines which subset each number belongs to
- `ssum`: the target sum for each subset, calculated by dividing the total sum of `s` by the number of subsets
The basic restrictions or rules of the problem are:
- The sum of the numbers in each subset must be equal
- The numbers in `s` must be divided into the specified number of subsets
The goal of the problem is to find a division of the numbers in `s` into subsets with equal sums, if such a division exists.
