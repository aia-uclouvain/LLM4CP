The other constraints represent the if-then statements that relate the symbols in the two sets.
objective: The objective of the problem is to find a satisfying assignment of values to the variables, such that all the constraints are satisfied. In other words, the objective is to find the correspondence between the two sets of symbols that satisfies all the given conditions.
