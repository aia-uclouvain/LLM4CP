Portfolio Optimization Problem
The problem is about investing money in different assets, like stocks, to get the best return while managing risk.
The main variables are: 
- x, which represents how much money to invest in each asset
- f, which represents the total risk of the investments
- r, which represents the expected return of each asset
- B, which is the total amount of money available to invest
- G, which is the minimum return required
The basic restrictions are: 
- the total amount invested cannot exceed the available money
- the total expected return must be at least the minimum required
- the risk of the investments must be calculated based on how much is invested in each asset and how they relate to each other
The goal is to minimize the total risk of the investments while meeting the minimum return requirement.
