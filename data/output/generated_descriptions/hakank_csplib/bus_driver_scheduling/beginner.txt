Bus Driver Scheduling 
The problem is about creating a schedule for bus drivers to cover all their tasks, like driving certain routes, while using the fewest number of shifts possible. 
The main variables are the tasks, which are the pieces of work the bus drivers need to do, and the shifts, which are the periods of time when a driver is working. 
The basic restrictions are that each task must be covered by exactly one shift, and each shift can cover multiple tasks. 
The goal is to minimize the number of shifts used to cover all tasks, which means finding the most efficient way to schedule the bus drivers.
