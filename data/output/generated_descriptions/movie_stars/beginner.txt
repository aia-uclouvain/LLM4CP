Movie Stars problem
The problem is about predicting how a person, named Evan, will rate a movie called "Prognosis Negative" based on how other people have rated the same movie and other movies. 
The main variables are the ratings given by different people to different movies, and the unknown rating that Evan will give to "Prognosis Negative". 
The basic restrictions are that each person's rating for a movie is between 1 and 5, and that we can only use the given ratings to make the prediction. 
The goal is to find the most likely rating that Evan will give to "Prognosis Negative" by comparing his ratings for other movies to the ratings given by other people.
