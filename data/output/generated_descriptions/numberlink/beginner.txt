Numberlink problem. 
The problem is about connecting pairs of numbers in a grid by drawing lines between them, such that each number is connected to its matching pair and the lines do not intersect. 
The main variables are the grid size (number of rows and columns), the number of pairs, and the connections between the numbers in the grid. 
The basic restrictions are that each number can only be connected to its matching pair, and the lines cannot intersect. 
The goal of the problem is to find a solution that connects all the pairs without any intersections.
