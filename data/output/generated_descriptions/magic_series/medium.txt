Magic Series Problem
The problem is about finding a set of distinct numbers within a given range that sum up to a specific target value, known as the magic constant.
The main decision variables are the elements of the array `x`, where `x[i]` represents the `i`-th number in the magic series, and its value ranges from 1 to `n^2`.
The key constraints are: 
- the sum of the numbers in the series must be equal to the magic constant `M_n = 1/2 * n * (n^2 + 1)`, 
- all numbers in the series must be distinct, 
- and the numbers must be in increasing order.
The objective of the problem is to find a magic series that satisfies these constraints, rather than minimizing or maximizing a specific value. In other words, the goal is to find a set of distinct numbers that sum up to the magic constant, without any specific optimization criterion.
