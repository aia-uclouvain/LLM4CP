name: Column Sum Puzzle
description: The Column Sum Puzzle is a mathematical puzzle where you are given a square matrix with some numbers already filled in, and the sum of each row, column, and diagonal. The goal is to fill in the remaining numbers in the matrix such that the sum of each row, column, and diagonal matches the given sums.
variables: $x_{i,j}$ for $i = 1, \ldots, n$ and $j = 1, \ldots, n$, where $x_{i,j}$ represents the value in the $i$-th row and $j$-th column of the matrix, and is a decision variable that can take on integer values between 1 and 9. 
constraints: $\sum_{j=1}^{n} x_{i,j} = \text{rowsums}_i$ for $i = 1, \ldots, n$, $\sum_{i=1}^{n} x_{i,j} = \text{colsums}_j$ for $j = 1, \ldots, n$, $\sum_{i=1}^{n} x_{i,i} = \text{diagsums}_1$, $\sum_{i=1}^{n} x_{i,n-i+1} = \text{diagsums}_2$, and $x_{i,j} = \text{clues}_{i,j}$ for $i = 1, \ldots, n$ and $j = 1, \ldots, n$ where $\text{clues}_{i,j} > 0$, which ensure that the sum of each row, column, and diagonal matches the given sums, and the given clues are respected.
objective: satisfy, which means the objective is to find a feasible solution that satisfies all the constraints, rather than minimizing or maximizing a specific value.
