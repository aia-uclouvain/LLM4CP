Car Replacement Problem
The problem is about deciding when to replace a car to minimize costs. 
The main variables are: 
- timex: the number of years to consider
- new_car: the cost of buying a new car
- trade_in: the price at which the old car can be sold
- maintenance: the cost of repairing the car each year
- x: a table showing the strategy to choose, where x[i,j] represents replacing the car at year i with a new one that will be used until year j
- cost: a table showing the cost of replacing the car at year i with a new one that will be used until year j
- z: the total cost of the chosen strategy
The basic restrictions are: 
- the cost of replacing the car at any time must be non-negative
- the cost of replacing the car is calculated based on the cost of the new car, the maintenance cost, and the trade-in value
- the number of cars bought and sold at each time must be balanced
The goal is to minimize the total cost z.
