The problem is called "Controversy about the weekday". 
It's about seven friends who can't agree on what day of the week it is. 
The main variables are "today" (the current day of the week) and "truth" (whether each friend's statement is true or not). 
The basic restrictions are that each friend makes a statement about the day of the week, and only one of these statements can be true. 
The goal is to figure out what day of the week the friends met, given their statements. 
For example, Klaus says it's Tuesday, Sabine says tomorrow is Tuesday, and so on. 
The problem wants to find the day of the week that makes only one of these statements true, and the rest false.
