The problem is called Alphametics. 
It's about solving a mathematical puzzle where letters represent numbers, and when you replace the letters with the correct numbers, the equation is true. 
The main variables are letters like A, N, C, E, L, R, T, I, G, and F, which represent digits from 0 to 9. 
The basic restrictions are that each letter must be a different digit, and when you plug these digits into the equation, it must be correct. 
The goal is to find the correct digits for each letter so that the equation is true, which means finding a solution that satisfies all the constraints.
