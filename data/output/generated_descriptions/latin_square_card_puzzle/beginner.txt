Latin Square Card Puzzle
The problem is about arranging a set of special cards in a square pattern. 
The main variables are the cards, which have two properties: a value (like a king or a queen) and a suit (like hearts or diamonds). 
The basic restrictions are that no value or suit can be repeated in any row, column, or diagonal of the square. 
The goal is to find a way to arrange all the cards in the square so that these rules are followed, which is a kind of puzzle.
