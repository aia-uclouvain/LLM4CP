The problem is called Weighted Sum. 
It is about finding a combination of numbers that when multiplied by certain weights and added together, give a specific total. 
The main variables are coeffs, which are the weights, x, which are the numbers, and s, which is the total. 
The basic restrictions are that each number (x) can only be between 0 and 5, and the total (s) must be equal to the weighted sum of the numbers. 
The goal of the problem is to find a combination of numbers that satisfy these restrictions, specifically to find the total (s) when the weights (coeffs) are [1, 2, -3] and the numbers (x) are between 0 and 5.
