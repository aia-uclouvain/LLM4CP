Fire Station Placement Problem
The problem is about deciding where to place fire stations in a city to cover all areas within a certain distance. 
The main variables are: 
- x, which represents whether a fire station is placed in a particular city (1 means yes, 0 means no)
- distance, which is a table showing how far each city is from every other city
- min_distance, which is the maximum distance a city can be from a fire station
- z, which is the total number of fire stations placed.
The basic restrictions are: 
- each city must be within the minimum distance of at least one fire station
- a fire station can only be placed in a city
- the distance between cities is fixed and known.
The goal is to minimize the total number of fire stations (z) needed to cover all cities.
