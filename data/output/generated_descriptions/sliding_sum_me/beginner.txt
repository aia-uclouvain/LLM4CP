Sliding Sum Problem
The problem is about finding a set of numbers that satisfy certain rules. 
The main variables are: 
- "variables" which is a list of numbers, 
- "low" and "up" which are the minimum and maximum allowed sums, 
- "seq" which is the length of a sequence of numbers to consider.
The basic restrictions are that the sum of any sequence of "seq" consecutive numbers in the list must be between "low" and "up". 
The goal is to find a list of numbers that satisfies these rules, without trying to minimize or maximize anything, just to find a solution that works.
