The problem is called Symmetric Graph. 
It's about creating a special kind of graph where if there's a connection from point A to point B, then there must also be a connection from point B to point A. 
The main variables are the points in the graph and the connections between them, which are like roads between cities. 
The basic rules are that for every connection from one point to another, there must be a connection in the opposite direction, and the graph must match a given example. 
The goal is to make sure the graph follows these rules, rather than trying to minimize or maximize something.
