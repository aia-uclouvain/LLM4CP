Einstein Puzzle
The problem is about figuring out the characteristics of five houses in a row, where each house has a different owner with a unique nationality, favorite color, pet, drink, and cigarette brand.
The main variables are the characteristics of each house: 
- Nationality (A): which nationality lives in each house
- Color (B): the color of each house
- Pet (C): the type of pet in each house
- Drink (D): the favorite drink of the owner in each house
- Smoke (E): the favorite cigarette brand of the owner in each house
The basic restrictions are that each house must have a unique combination of characteristics, and there are specific clues that relate the characteristics of one house to another, such as "the person who owns a cat lives next to the person who drinks milk".
The goal of the problem is to figure out the characteristics of each house, specifically to determine which nationality owns the fish.
