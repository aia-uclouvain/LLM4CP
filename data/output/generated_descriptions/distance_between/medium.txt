The name of the problem is Distance Between.
This problem is about measuring the distance between two sets of variables, x and y, based on their relative orderings.
The main decision variables are x and y, which are arrays of variables representing the two sets, and d, which represents the distance between these sets. 
The key constraints are that the distance d is calculated by comparing the relative orderings of corresponding elements in x and y. Specifically, for each pair of elements in x and y, if the ordering of the elements in x is less than the ordering of the elements in y, but the reverse is not true, or vice versa, it contributes to the distance d.
The problem's objective is to maximize the distance d, which means finding the maximum number of times the relative orderings of corresponding elements in x and y are inconsistent.
