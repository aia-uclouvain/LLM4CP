The problem is called Distance Between. 
It's about comparing two lists of numbers, x and y, to see how many times the relationship between two numbers in one list is different from the relationship between the corresponding numbers in the other list. 
The main variables are x and y, which are lists of numbers, and d, which is the distance or the number of times the relationships are different. 
The basic restrictions are that x and y must be lists of the same length, and the relationships between numbers in x and y are compared using a specific operator, in this case, less than. 
The goal of the problem is to maximize the distance d, which means finding the highest number of times the relationships between numbers in x and y are different.
