The problem is called "Another Kind of Magic Square". 
It's about arranging numbers in a special square so that the numbers around each number add up to a multiple of that number. 
The main variables are the numbers in the square, which are represented by "magic". 
The basic restrictions are that each number in the square must be different, and the numbers surrounding each number must add up to a multiple of that number. 
The goal is to find a way to arrange the numbers in the square that satisfies these rules, and the model uses a method to search for the solution that tries to assign the smallest possible numbers to the variables first.
