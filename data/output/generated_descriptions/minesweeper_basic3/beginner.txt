Minesweeper
The problem is about a popular puzzle game where you have to figure out where mines are hidden in a grid. 
The main variables are the positions in the grid, which can either be a mine (represented by X) or a number showing how many mines are nearby.
The basic restrictions are that the numbers in the grid must be correct, meaning they must show the correct number of mines adjacent to them. 
The goal is to correctly identify all the mines in the grid, which is like solving the puzzle.
