The problem is called Sortedness. 
It's about taking a list of numbers and sorting them in order from smallest to largest. 
The main variables are: 
- x, which is the original list of numbers, 
- s, which is the sorted list of numbers, and 
- p, which shows how to rearrange the original list to get the sorted list. 
The basic rules are: 
- all numbers in the original list must be different, 
- the second number in the original list must be 5, 
- the fourth number in the sorted list must be 10, and 
- the sorted list must be a rearrangement of the original list. 
The goal is to find a solution that satisfies all these rules.
