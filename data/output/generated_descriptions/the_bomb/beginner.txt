The Bomb puzzle 
The problem is about disarming a bomb with 7 flip switches by putting them in the correct positions to avoid the bomb detonating. 
The main variables are the 7 switches, which can be either on or off. 
The basic restrictions or rules of the problem are that certain combinations of switch positions will cause the bomb to detonate, such as having switch 3 on and switches 2 and 4 off. 
The goal of the problem is to find a combination of switch positions that will not cause the bomb to detonate, which means finding a solution that satisfies all the given conditions and avoids all the "boom" scenarios.
