Peg Solitaire
The problem is about a game where you have a board with pegs and holes, and you need to jump pegs over each other to remove them from the board. 
The main variables are the state of the board at each time step (x) and the moves made at each time step (y). 
The basic restrictions are that you can only jump a peg over another peg to an empty hole, and you can't move a peg to a hole that already has a peg. 
The goal is to find a sequence of moves that transforms the initial board into the final board in a given number of steps.
