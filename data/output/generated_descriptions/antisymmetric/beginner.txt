Antisymmetric Relation Problem
The problem is about finding a relationship between different things, where if one thing is related to another, the second thing cannot be related to the first. 
The main variable is x, which is a table that shows the relationships between different things. In this table, a 1 means that the thing on the left is related to the thing on the top, and a 0 means they are not related.
The basic rules are that no thing can be related to itself, and if one thing is related to another, the second thing cannot be related to the first.
The goal of the problem is to find a relationship that follows these rules, without trying to minimize or maximize anything, just to satisfy the conditions.
