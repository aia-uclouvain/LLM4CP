Zebra Ice Cream Problem
The problem is about figuring out the favorite ice cream flavors of four boys who are standing side by side. 
The main variables are the boys' first names, last names, shirt colors, favorite ice cream flavors, ages, and positions in line. 
The basic restrictions are that each boy has a unique first name, last name, shirt color, favorite ice cream flavor, age, and position in line. 
The goal is to find out who likes mint ice cream by satisfying all the given conditions, such as the oldest boy wearing a blue shirt, Nicholas standing next to the boy who likes strawberry ice cream, and other rules.
