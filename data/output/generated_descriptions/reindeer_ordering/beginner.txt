Reindeer Ordering 
The problem is about determining the order in which nine reindeer will pull Santa's sleigh. 
The main variables are the names of the reindeer, which represent their positions in the line. 
The basic restrictions or rules of the problem are that each reindeer must be in a specific position relative to the others, such as "Comet behind Rudolph, Prancer, and Cupid" or "Blitzen in front of Donder, Vixen, and Dancer". 
The goal of the problem is to find an order for the reindeer that satisfies all of these rules.
