P-median problem
The problem is about deciding where to build warehouses to serve customers in a way that minimizes the total distance customers have to travel to get to a warehouse, taking into account how much each customer needs.

The main variables are:
- OpenWarehouse: whether a warehouse is built at a certain location
- ShipToCustomer: which warehouse each customer gets their goods from
- Demand: how much each customer needs
- Distance: how far each customer is from each possible warehouse location
- z: the total distance all customers have to travel to get to a warehouse

The basic restrictions or rules are:
- Each customer must get their goods from one and only one warehouse
- Only a certain number (P) of warehouses can be built
- A customer can only get their goods from a warehouse that has been built

The goal is to minimize the total distance all customers have to travel to get to a warehouse, taking into account how much each customer needs.
