The problem is called "Languages at a Round Table". 
It's about 5 people from different countries - France, England, Poland, Italy, and Turkey - sitting at a round table. Each person can speak their own language and one other language. 
The main variables are the people's native languages and the foreign languages they know. 
The basic rules are that each person can talk to the people sitting next to them, one in their own language and one in the foreign language they know. 
The goal is to figure out what foreign language the Turkish person knows, given some clues about who knows what language.
