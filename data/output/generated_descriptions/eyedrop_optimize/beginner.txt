Eye Drop Optimization Problem
The problem is about finding the best way to take eye drops after a cataract operation. 
The main variables are the times when each type of eye drop should be taken, and the goal is to spread out these times as much as possible.
The basic restrictions are that there are three types of eye drops to be taken a certain number of times per day, and each type should be taken at evenly spaced times. 
The goal is to maximize the dispersion of the times when each type of eye drop is taken, which means spreading out the times as much as possible.
