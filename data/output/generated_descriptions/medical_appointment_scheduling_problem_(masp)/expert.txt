name: Medical Appointment Scheduling Problem (MASP)
description: The Medical Appointment Scheduling Problem is a complex scheduling problem that involves assigning medical appointments to available time slots and resources, such as doctors and medical specialties, while satisfying various constraints and preferences, including patient preferred dates, times, and doctors.
variables: $appSlots, appResources, auxVarCalendars$ 
These variables represent the decision variables in the problem: $appSlots$ is an array of integer variables representing the time slots assigned to each appointment, $appResources$ is an array of integer variables representing the resources (doctors or medical specialties) assigned to each appointment, and $auxVarCalendars$ is an array of auxiliary variables used to link resources to appointment time slots.
constraints: $appSlots \geq 0, appSlots \in \text{global calendar}, appResources \in \text{resource domains}, \text{allDifferent}(appSlots), \text{allDifferent}(appResources), appSlots[i+1] = appSlots[i] + 1, \text{linkResourcesToAppointmentFirstSlotConstraint}, \text{eliminateUndesiredDates}, \text{optimizePreferredDates}, \text{optimizePreferredDoctors}, \text{optimizePreferredTimes}$
These constraints ensure that the appointment scheduling is valid and satisfies various requirements, including: time slots are non-negative and within the global calendar, resources are assigned from their respective domains, time slots and resources are distinct, consecutive time slots are assigned to the same appointment, resources are linked to appointment time slots, undesired dates are eliminated, and patient preferences for dates, doctors, and times are optimized.
objective: Minimize the total number of violations of patient preferences, including preferred dates, doctors, and times, represented by the variable $optimizeSoftConstraints$.
