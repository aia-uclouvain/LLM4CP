Shopping Basket Optimization Problem
The problem is about finding the best way to buy a list of items from different shops to get the lowest total cost. 
The main variables are the items to buy, the shops that sell these items, and the prices of the items at each shop. 
The basic restrictions are that each item must be bought from one shop, and if the total cost of items from a shop is below a certain limit, a delivery cost is added. 
The goal is to minimize the total cost, which includes the prices of the items and any delivery costs.
