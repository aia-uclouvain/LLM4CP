Conference Scheduling Problem
The problem is about scheduling talks for a conference with multiple speakers, each available at certain time slots, to finish all talks as early as possible.
The main variables are: 
- starting_slot: the time slot when a speaker starts their talk
- ending_slot: the time slot when a speaker finishes their talk
- speaker_availability: the time slots when a speaker is available to give their talk
- app_durations: the duration of each speaker's talk
The basic restrictions are: 
- a speaker can only give their talk during their available time slots
- no two speakers can give their talks at the same time
- each speaker must give their talk for the predetermined duration
The goal is to minimize the time when all talks finish, i.e., to finish all talks as early as possible.
