Soccer Tournament Problem
The problem is about a soccer tournament where five teams play against each other once. 
The main variables are the number of points each team gets and the result of each game between two teams. 
In simple terms, the number of points a team gets is based on whether they win, tie, or lose a game, and the result of each game is either a win (3 points), a tie (1 point), or a loss (0 points). 
The basic restrictions are that each team plays every other team once, a team can't play against itself, and the points for each game are awarded based on the result. 
The goal is to find out how many points the fifth team got, given that the other four teams got 1, 2, 5, and 7 points.
