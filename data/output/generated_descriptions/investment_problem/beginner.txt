Investment Problem
The problem is about a person who has $100 million to invest in different opportunities. 
The main variables are: 
- x: the amount of money invested in each opportunity, 
- the_returns: the return on investment for each opportunity, 
- not_invested: the amount of money not invested in any opportunity, 
- z: the total return on investment.
The basic restrictions are: 
- the total amount invested cannot exceed $100 million, 
- the amount invested in each opportunity must be between $0 and $40 million, 
- the amount invested must be a multiple of $10 million.
The goal is to maximize the total return on investment, which is the sum of the returns from each opportunity plus the return from any uninvested money.
