Farm Puzzle
The problem is about a farmer who has a certain number of pigs and hens on his farm, and we need to figure out how many of each animal he has. 
The main variables are the number of legs for each animal, which can be either 2 legs for a hen or 4 legs for a pig.
The basic restriction is that all the animals together have a total of 22 legs. 
The goal is to find a combination of pigs and hens that adds up to 22 legs in total, which will tell us how many pigs and hens the farmer has.
