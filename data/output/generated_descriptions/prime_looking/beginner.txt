Prime Looking Problem 
This problem is about finding numbers that are not prime but seem like they could be, because they are not divisible by 2, 3, or 5. 
The main variables are x, which is the number we're looking at, and d1 and d2, which are the factors of x that multiply together to give x.
The basic rules are that x must be a product of two numbers (d1 and d2), and x cannot be divisible by 2, 3, or 5. 
The goal is to find how many numbers like this exist that are less than 1000.
