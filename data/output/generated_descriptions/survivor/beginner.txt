Survivor problem
The problem is about a person who is lost in the wilderness and needs to choose the right items to take with them to survive. 
The main variables are the items to choose from, including food, water, shelter, and defense, and the goal is to select one item from each category. 
The basic restrictions are that the total weight of the chosen items cannot exceed 25 kg, and each item has a certain weight and survival points. 
The goal of the problem is to maximize the total survival points, which means choosing the items that will give the person the best chance of surviving.
