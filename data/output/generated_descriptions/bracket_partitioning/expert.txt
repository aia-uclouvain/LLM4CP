name: Bracket Partitioning Problem
description: The problem is to partition a set of participants into brackets, where each participant has a weight associated with them. The goal is to create brackets with a maximum of 15% difference between the lowest and highest weighted participants, while favoring brackets of size 4 and 8.
variables: $x, set\_size, mins, maxs, diffs, z$ 
These variables represent the following: 
- $x$: an array of decision variables representing which bracket each participant belongs to, 
- $set\_size$: an array of decision variables representing the size of each bracket, 
- $mins$ and $maxs$: arrays of decision variables representing the minimum and maximum weights in each bracket, 
- $diffs$: an array of decision variables representing the difference between the maximum and minimum weights in each bracket, 
- $z$: a decision variable representing the total number of brackets of size 4 and 8.
constraints: $\begin{aligned} 
& global\_cardinality(x, [i | i \in 1..ssize], set\_size) \\
& min\_except\_0(mins[s],[w[i]*bool2int(x[i]=s) | i \in 1..n]) \\
& maxs[s] = max([w[i]*bool2int(x[i]=s) | i \in 1..n]) \\
& diffs[s] = maxs[s] - mins[s] \\
& 10000*diffs[s] div maxs[s] \leq 1500 \\
& sum(set\_size) = n \\
& value\_precede\_chain([ i | i \in 1..ssize], x) \\
& x[1] = 1 \\
& decreasing(set\_size) \\
& set\_size[s] = sum(j \in 1..n) ( bool2int(x[j]=s))
\end{aligned}$ 
These constraints represent the following: 
- Each participant is assigned to a bracket, 
- The minimum and maximum weights in each bracket are calculated, 
- The difference between the maximum and minimum weights in each bracket does not exceed 15%, 
- The total number of participants is equal to the sum of the sizes of all brackets, 
- The brackets are numbered in a way that ensures a participant is not assigned to a bracket with a higher number than a participant who is not assigned to any bracket with a lower number, 
- The first participant is assigned to the first bracket, 
- The sizes of the brackets are in decreasing order, 
- The size of each bracket is equal to the number of participants assigned to it.
objective: Maximize $z$, the total number of brackets of size 4 and 8.
