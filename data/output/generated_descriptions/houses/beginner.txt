The problem is called the "Houses problem". 
It's about figuring out who lives in each of 8 houses on a street, given some clues about the neighbors and locations of 8 people: Allison, Adrienne, Belinda, Benito, Cheri, Crawford, Daryl, and Don. 
The main variables are the house numbers of each person, which are like addresses. 
The basic restrictions are: 
- Allison lives next to Adrienne and in a house with a number greater than 2. 
- Belinda lives two houses away from Benito and in a house with a number greater than 5. 
- Cheri lives three houses away from Crawford and in a house with a number greater than Benito's. 
- Daryl lives four houses away from Don and in a house with a number less than 4. 
- Each person lives in a different house. 
The goal is to find out who lives in each house, given these restrictions.
