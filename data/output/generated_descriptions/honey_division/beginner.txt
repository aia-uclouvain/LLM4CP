Honey Division Puzzle
The problem is about dividing honey among sons using barrels of different capacities. 
The main variables are the number of barrels each son gets of each capacity, and how full each barrel is. 
The basic restrictions are that each son must get 7 barrels and 3.5 units of honey, and each barrel capacity must be used 7 times. 
The goal is to find a way to divide the honey that satisfies these rules.
