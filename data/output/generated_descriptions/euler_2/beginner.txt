Fibonacci Sum Problem
The problem is about finding the sum of certain numbers in a special sequence called the Fibonacci sequence. 
The main variables are: 
- f, which represents the numbers in the Fibonacci sequence, 
- x, which helps identify whether each number in the sequence should be included in the sum, 
- res, which is the total sum of the included numbers.
The basic restrictions are: 
- each number in the sequence is the sum of the two numbers before it, 
- only numbers that are even (can be divided by 2 without a remainder) and less than 4 million should be included in the sum.
The goal is to find the sum of all these even numbers in the sequence that do not exceed 4 million.
