The problem is called "Persistent Numbers". 
It's about finding the smallest number that takes a certain number of steps to reduce to a single digit by multiplying its digits together. 
The main variables are the number to start with and the digits of the numbers at each step. 
The basic restrictions are that the digits must be multiplied together to get the next number, and this process must be repeated until a single digit is reached. 
The goal is to find the smallest number that takes a specific number of steps, in this case, five steps, to reduce to a single digit.
