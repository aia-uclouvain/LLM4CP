The problem is called the Open Alldifferent problem. 
It's about having a list of numbers and a special set of positions in that list, and making sure that the numbers at those special positions are all different from each other. 
The main variables are x, which is the list of numbers, and s, which is the set of special positions. 
The basic restrictions are that the list of numbers must match a given example list, and the numbers at the special positions must be different. 
The goal is to find a list of numbers and a set of special positions that satisfy these restrictions, which is done by solving the problem.
