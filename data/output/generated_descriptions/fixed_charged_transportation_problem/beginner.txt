Fixed-Charge Transportation Problem
The problem is about finding the cheapest way to transport goods from sources to customers. 
The main variables are the amount of goods shipped from each source to each customer and whether a shipping route is used. 
The basic restrictions are that each source can only ship a certain amount of goods, each customer needs a certain amount of goods, and shipping a certain amount of goods on a route costs a fixed amount plus a cost per unit shipped. 
The goal is to minimize the total cost of shipping all the goods.
