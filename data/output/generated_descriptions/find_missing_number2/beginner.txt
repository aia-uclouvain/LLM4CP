Missing Number Problem
The problem is about finding a missing number from a concatenated string of numbers from 1 to 250 in random order. 
The main variables are the missing number, the digits of the missing number, and the positions of the numbers in the concatenated string. 
The basic restrictions are that the missing number is between 1 and 250, and its digits must match the difference between the sum of all digits of numbers 1-250 and the sum of digits in the given string. 
The goal is to find the missing number by satisfying these constraints.
