These constraints ensure that each nut has a unique arrangement of numbers, the numbers on the sides of each nut match the numbers on the adjoining nut, the center nut starts with 1, and the connections between the nuts are satisfied.
objective: satisfy 
The objective of the problem is to find a feasible solution that satisfies all the constraints, as there is no explicit objective function to minimize or maximize.
