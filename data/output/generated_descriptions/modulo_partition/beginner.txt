The problem is called Function Partitions. 
It's about dividing a set of numbers (from 1 to a certain number, n) into groups based on a specific rule, like remainder when divided by another number. 
The main variables are: x (the groups of numbers), sums (the sum of numbers in each group), and x_bool (a way to show which numbers are in which groups). 
The basic restrictions are that each number can only be in one group, and the groups are determined by the rule (like remainder when divided by another number). 
The goal is to find the groups and their sums, and show which numbers are in which groups.
