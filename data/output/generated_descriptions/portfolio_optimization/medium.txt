Portfolio Optimization Problem
The problem is about investing a given amount of money in a set of assets to maximize returns while minimizing risk.
The main decision variables are `x`, an array of variables representing the amount of money invested in each asset, and `f`, a variable representing the total risk of the portfolio.
The key constraints are: 
- the total amount invested in all assets cannot exceed a given budget `B`, 
- the total return of the portfolio must be greater than or equal to a given threshold `G`, 
- the risk of the portfolio, calculated as the sum of the products of the amounts invested in each pair of assets and their corresponding covariance coefficients, must be minimized.
The objective of the problem is to minimize the total risk `f` of the portfolio, which is a measure of the uncertainty or volatility of the portfolio's returns.
