The problem is called the Broken Weights problem. 
It's about a merchant who had a 40-pound weight that broke into four pieces, and each piece weighs a whole number of pounds. 
The main variables are the weights of the four pieces, which are like the building blocks to measure other weights. 
The basic restrictions are that the total weight of the four pieces must be 40 pounds, and that the pieces can be used to measure every whole weight from 1 to 40 pounds using a balance scale, where a piece can be on either side of the scale. 
The goal is to find the weights of the four pieces that can measure all these weights, and to do so in a way that minimizes the weight of the heaviest piece.
