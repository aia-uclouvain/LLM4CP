The problem is known as the "Smallest Number Divisible by 225 with Only 1s and 0s" or the "225 Divisor Problem". 
This problem is about finding the smallest number that consists only of the digits 1 and 0 and is divisible by 225. 
The main decision variables are x, an array of binary variables (0 or 1) representing the digits of the number, and y, a variable representing the actual number formed by the digits in x. 
The key constraints are that the number y must be formed by concatenating the digits in x, and y must be divisible by 225. 
The problem's objective is to minimize y, meaning to find the smallest possible number that meets the given conditions.
