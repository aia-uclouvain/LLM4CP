**Problem Name:** Shopping with Delivery Cost Problem

**Description:** The problem involves buying different kinds of parts from various stores while minimizing the total cost, including delivery costs. Each store may or may not have a specific part, and the goal is to find the optimal combination of stores to buy from to minimize the total cost.

**Decision Variables:** The main decision variable is `x`, a 2D array where `x[p]` represents the store from which to buy part `p`. The value of `x[p]` is an integer between 1 and `num_stores`, indicating the store index.

**Key Constraints:**

* A selected part must be available in the chosen store, i.e., the price of the part in the store must be greater than 0.
* The total cost of the parts is calculated by summing the prices of each part in the chosen store.
* The delivery cost is calculated by summing the delivery costs of the stores from which at least one part is bought.

**Objective:** The objective is to minimize the total cost, which is the sum of the part costs and the delivery costs. The model aims to find the optimal assignment of parts to stores that results in the minimum total cost.
