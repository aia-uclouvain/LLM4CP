Primes in a Circle 
This problem is about arranging the numbers 1 to 14 in a circle so that the sum and difference of any two numbers next to each other are prime numbers. 
The main variables are the numbers 1 to 14, which will be placed in a circle. 
The basic restrictions are that each number can only be used once, and the sum and difference of any two neighboring numbers must be prime. 
The goal is to find a valid arrangement of the numbers 1 to 14 in a circle that satisfies these conditions.
