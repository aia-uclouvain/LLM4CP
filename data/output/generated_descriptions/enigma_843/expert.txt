name: Enigma Problem 843 (How many are whole?)

The problem consists of four main numbers: ONE, TWO, THREE, and FOUR, and the task is to determine the values of these numbers and a fifth number, FORTUNE.

These constraints ensure that each digit is unique, the numbers are formed correctly, and the conditions for perfect squares are met.
objective: The objective of the problem is to satisfy all the given constraints, as it is a constraint satisfaction problem, and the goal is to find a solution that meets all the conditions, rather than minimizing or maximizing a specific value.
