The problem is called Global Cardinality. 
It's about making sure that certain numbers appear a specific number of times in a list. 
The main variables are "variables" which is a list of numbers, and "values" which is a table that says how many times each number should appear. 
The basic restrictions are that the numbers in the list must match the counts in the table, and every number in the list must be between 1 and 8. 
The goal is to find a list of numbers that satisfies these conditions, and to show how the numbers and their counts match up.
