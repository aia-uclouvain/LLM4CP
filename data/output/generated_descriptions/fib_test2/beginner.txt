Fibonacci Numbers Problem
The problem is about calculating a series of numbers called Fibonacci numbers, which is a sequence where each number is the sum of the two preceding ones. 
The main variable is "fib", which is an array of numbers that will store the Fibonacci sequence.
The basic restrictions are that the first two numbers in the sequence must be 1, and each subsequent number must be the sum of the two preceding ones.
The goal of the problem is to calculate the first 11 numbers in the Fibonacci sequence.
