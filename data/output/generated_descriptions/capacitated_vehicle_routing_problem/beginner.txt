Capacitated Vehicle Routing Problem
The problem is about delivering goods from a central location (called a depot) to several other locations using a fleet of vehicles, each with a limited capacity. 
The main variables are: 
- the locations where the goods need to be delivered 
- the vehicles that will transport the goods 
- the routes that each vehicle will take to deliver the goods 
- the amount of goods that each vehicle can carry.
The basic restrictions or rules are: 
- each vehicle can only carry a certain amount of goods 
- each location must receive its goods 
- the vehicles must start and end at the depot 
- each vehicle can only make one trip.
The goal of the problem is to find the most efficient way to deliver all the goods while minimizing the total distance traveled by the vehicles.
