All Different Pairs Problem
The problem is about creating all possible pairs of numbers from a given set of numbers, without repeating any pair, and arranging them in a specific order. 
The main variables are the pairs of numbers, where each pair consists of two numbers from the set. 
The basic restrictions are that each pair must be unique, the pairs must be in increasing order, and the two numbers in each pair cannot be the same. 
The goal of the problem is to find all possible combinations of pairs that satisfy these conditions, and to list them out in a specific format, including their integer representation.
