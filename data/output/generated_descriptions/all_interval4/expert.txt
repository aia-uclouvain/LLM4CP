description: The All Interval Problem is a well-known problem in music theory and combinatorial optimization. It involves finding a sequence of musical notes, represented by integers, such that each note appears exactly once and the differences between consecutive notes are all different. This problem has applications in music composition, as it can be used to generate musical sequences with unique and interesting properties.
