Seven11 puzzle
The problem is about finding four numbers that add up to 711 and when multiplied together result in 711 multiplied by 100^3. 
The main variables are four numbers, x, which represent the four unknown values that need to be found.
The basic restrictions are that the four numbers must add up to 711, their product must be 711 * 100^3, and they must be in increasing order.
The goal of the problem is to find a set of four numbers that satisfy these conditions, with no specific objective to minimize or maximize, but rather to find a solution that meets all the given constraints.
