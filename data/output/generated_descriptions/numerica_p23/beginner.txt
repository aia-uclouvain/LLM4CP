Robot Kinematic Application 
This problem is about finding the position of a robot's joints. 
The main variables are s and c, which represent the positions of the robot's joints in terms of sine and cosine values. 
The basic restrictions are that each joint's position must be within a certain range and that the robot's joints must satisfy certain mathematical equations that describe how the joints are connected. 
The goal of the problem is to find all possible positions of the robot's joints that satisfy these equations, with a high degree of precision.
