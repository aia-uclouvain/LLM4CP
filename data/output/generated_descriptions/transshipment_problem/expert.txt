name: Transportation Problem with Transshipment
description: The problem involves finding the optimal way to transport items from warehouses to customers through transshipment points, while minimizing the total transportation cost and satisfying the demand of each customer, without exceeding the available stock at each warehouse.
variables: $amountWT_{w,t}, amountTC_{t,c}$, where $amountWT_{w,t}$ represents the amount of items transported from warehouse $w$ to transshipment point $t$, and $amountTC_{t,c}$ represents the amount of items transported from transshipment point $t$ to customer $c$. These variables determine the flow of items through the transportation network.
constraints: $\sum_{w} amountWT_{w,t} = \sum_{c} amountTC_{t,c} \forall t$, $\sum_{t} amountTC_{t,c} = demand_c \forall c$, $\sum_{t} amountWT_{w,t} \leq stock_w \forall w$, where the first constraint preserves the flow of items at each transshipment point, the second constraint ensures that the demand of each customer is met, and the third constraint ensures that the available stock at each warehouse is not exceeded.
objective: The objective is to minimize the total transportation cost, $totalCost = \sum_{w,t} costWT_{w,t} \cdot amountWT_{w,t} + \sum_{t,c} costTC_{t,c} \cdot amountTC_{t,c}$, which is the sum of the costs of transporting items from warehouses to transshipment points and from transshipment points to customers.
