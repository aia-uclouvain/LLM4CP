Transportation Problem
The problem is about finding the cheapest way to transport goods from factories to markets. 
The main variables are the amount of goods shipped from each factory to each market, and the total cost of shipping.
The basic restrictions are that each factory can only ship a certain amount of goods, and each market needs to receive a certain amount of goods. 
The goal is to minimize the total cost of shipping while meeting the demands of the markets and not exceeding the supply limits of the factories.
