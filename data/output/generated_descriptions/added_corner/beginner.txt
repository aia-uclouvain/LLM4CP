Added Corner Puzzle 
The problem is about filling in numbers in a special pattern of circles and squares so that the numbers in the squares are the sum of the numbers in the circles around them. 
The main variables are A, B, C, D, E, F, G, and H, which represent the numbers to be placed in each circle and square.
The basic restrictions are that each number from 1 to 8 can only be used once, and the numbers in the squares must be the sum of the numbers in the circles that touch them.
The goal is to find a way to fill in the numbers so that all the rules are followed, and the puzzle is solved.
