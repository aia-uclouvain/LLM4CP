Office Blocked problem
The problem is about figuring out where six employees work in a two-floor office building with three rooms on each floor. 
The main variables are the floor and room numbers for each employee: Arthur, Bella, Craig, Duncan, Elizabeth, and Francesca. 
The basic restrictions are: Arthur works directly above Bella, Bella works next to Duncan, Elizabeth's office number is smaller than Francesca's, and Craig and Francesca work in adjacent rooms. 
The goal is to find out who works in which room on which floor.
