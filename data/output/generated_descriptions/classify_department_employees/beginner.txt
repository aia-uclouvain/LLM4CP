Employee Salary Statistics Problem
The problem is about analyzing the salaries of employees. 
The main variables are: 
- max_salaries: the highest salary among all employees
- min_salaries: the lowest salary among all employees
- avg_salaries: the average salary of all employees
- high_paid: a list of employees who earn more than $85,000
- num_high_paid: the number of employees who earn more than $85,000
The basic restrictions or rules of the problem are: 
- each employee has a salary
- an employee is considered "high-paid" if their salary is more than $85,000
The goal of the problem is to calculate the minimum, maximum, and average salaries, as well as the number of high-paid employees.
