Name of the problem: Among Seq

The problem is about finding a sequence of numbers that meets certain conditions. Imagine you have a list of numbers, and you want to check if there are enough numbers in each group of consecutive numbers that match certain values.

Main variables: 
- x is a list of numbers that we are examining.
- y is a list of specific numbers that we are looking for in x.
- low and up are the minimum and maximum number of times we want to find the numbers from y in each group of consecutive numbers in x.
- seq is the size of each group of consecutive numbers in x.

Basic restrictions or rules: 
- We are looking at groups of consecutive numbers in x that are seq numbers long.
- In each group, we want to find at least low numbers that are in y, and at most up numbers that are in y.

Goal of the problem: 
The goal is to check if the given list x and the specific numbers y satisfy the conditions of having at least low and at most up numbers from y in each group of consecutive numbers in x. The problem is not trying to minimize or maximize anything, but rather to find a solution that satisfies these conditions.
