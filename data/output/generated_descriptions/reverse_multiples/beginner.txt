Reverse Multiples Problem
The problem is about finding two numbers that, when multiplied by a certain multiplier, result in each other's reverse. 
The main variables are: 
- num1 and num2, which are the two numbers, 
- m, which is the multiplier, 
- a1 and a2, which represent the digits of num1 and num2, 
- n, which is the number of digits in num1 and num2, 
- base, which is the base of the number system (like 10 for decimal).
The basic restrictions are: 
- num2 multiplied by m equals num1, 
- num2 is less than num1, 
- a1 and a2 represent the digits of num1 and num2 in the given base, 
- a1 is the reverse of a2.
The goal is to find values for num1, num2, m, a1, and a2 that satisfy these restrictions.
