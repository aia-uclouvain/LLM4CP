name: Restaurant Scheduling Problem

description: The problem involves scheduling employees in a restaurant. The restaurant needs a specific number of employees each day of the week, and each employee works for a certain number of consecutive days followed by a certain number of days off. The goal is to determine the optimal number of employees to hire and their schedules to minimize the total cost.

variables: $x_i, working_i$ where $i = 1, \ldots, 7$ 
These variables represent the number of employees hired on day $i$ ($x_i$) and the total number of employees working on day $i$ ($working_i$).

constraints: $\sum_{w=d}^{d+work\_days-1} x_w + \sum_{w=1}^{d-work\_days} x_w \geq needed_d$ for $d = 1, \ldots, 7$, $x_i \geq 0$ for $i = 1, \ldots, 7$, $working_d = \sum_{w=d}^{d+work\_days-1} x_w + \sum_{w=1}^{d-work\_days} x_w$ for $d = 1, \ldots, 7$
These constraints ensure that the total number of employees working on each day is greater than or equal to the number of employees needed on that day, that the number of employees hired on each day is non-negative, and that the total number of employees working on each day is calculated correctly.

objective: Minimize $\sum_{i=1}^{7} work\_days \* cost\_per\_day \* x_i$
The objective is to minimize the total cost, which is calculated as the sum of the cost of hiring employees on each day, where the cost of hiring an employee on a given day is the number of days the employee works multiplied by the cost per day.
