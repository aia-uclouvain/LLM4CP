Movie Scheduling Problem
The problem is about scheduling movies to be shown on a single screen, given their start and end times, so that no two movies overlap. 
The main variables are the movies and their corresponding start and end times, and a variable x that decides whether a movie is shown or not. 
The basic restrictions are that no two movies can be shown at the same time, meaning their start and end times cannot overlap. 
The goal is to maximize the number of movies that can be shown, which is represented by the variable z.
