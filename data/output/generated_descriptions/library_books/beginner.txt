Library Books problem 
The problem is about three students, Arthur, Belinda, and Clarissa, who have overdue books from the library. 
The main variables are the students' names (Arthur, Belinda, Clarissa), the subjects they are majoring in (English, French, German), and the number of overdue books each student has. 
The basic restrictions are that the total number of overdue books is 25, no student has more than 14 overdue books, the French major has 3 more overdue books than Arthur, and Clarissa has 2 more overdue books than the English major. 
The goal is to figure out who is majoring in what subject and how many books each student needs to return to the library.
