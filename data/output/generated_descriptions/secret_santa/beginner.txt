Secret Santa problem
The problem is about a group of friends who want to play a game where each person gives gifts to another person in secret. 
The main variables are the people in the group, identified by numbers, and the families these people belong to, also identified by numbers. 
The basic restrictions are that a person cannot give gifts to themselves, and people in the same family cannot give gifts to each other. 
The goal is to find a way to assign each person to give gifts to another person, following these rules, so that everyone gives and receives gifts.
