Cookie Bake Off problem
The problem is about finding the perfect recipe for cookies by analyzing past attempts. 
The main variables are the amounts of sugar, flour, and butter used in the recipe, and the differences between the actual scores and the predicted scores.
The basic restrictions are that the total amount of ingredients is 8 cups, and the scores range from 0 to 100. 
The goal is to minimize the total difference between the actual scores and the predicted scores, which will help in finding the best recipe to achieve a perfect score of 100.
