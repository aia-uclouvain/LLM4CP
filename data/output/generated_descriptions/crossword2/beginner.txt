Crossword Puzzle Problem
The problem is about filling in a crossword puzzle with given words. 
The main variables are the words to be placed in the puzzle, represented by numbers 1 to 8, and the letters in each word, represented by numbers 1 to 26 (a to z).
The basic restrictions are that each word must be placed in the correct position, and the overlapping letters between words must be the same. 
The goal of the problem is to find a solution that satisfies all the constraints, filling in the crossword puzzle correctly.
