Farm Puzzle
The problem is about a farmer who has a certain number of pigs and hens on his farm, and we need to figure out how many of each animal he has.
The main variables are the number of pigs and hens, represented by the array "x" which shows the type of each animal (either 2 legs for a hen or 4 legs for a pig).
The basic restrictions are that there are 7 animals in total and they have a total of 22 legs, and each pig has 4 legs while each hen has 2 legs.
The goal of the problem is to find the number of pigs and hens that adds up to 7 animals and 22 legs, and to do this in a way that gives a unique solution by following a specific order.
