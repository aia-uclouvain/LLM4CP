Name of the problem: Among Modulo

The problem is about counting how many numbers in a list are even or odd, depending on certain rules. 

The main variables are: 
- x, which is a list of numbers
- nvar, which counts how many numbers in the list follow a certain rule
- remainder, which is a target value that the numbers in the list should leave when divided by another number
- quotient, which is the number by which we divide the numbers in the list to check the rule

The basic restrictions or rules are: 
- The numbers in the list x must be between 1 and 8.
- The numbers in the list must match a given list of numbers.
- We divide each number in the list by the quotient and check if the remainder is equal to the target remainder.

The goal of the problem is to find out how many numbers in the list leave a remainder of 0 when divided by 2, which means finding how many even numbers are in the list.
