Furniture Moving Problem
The problem is about moving furniture with a limited number of people. 
The main variables are: 
- the number of people (numPersons) available to move the furniture, 
- the start time (Starts) for each piece of furniture, 
- the time it takes (Durations) to move each piece of furniture, 
- the number of people (Resources) required to move each piece of furniture, 
- the end time (EndTimes) for each piece of furniture, and 
- the overall end time (end_time) for all the furniture.
The basic restrictions are: 
- each piece of furniture needs a certain number of people to move it, 
- the total number of people available is limited, 
- the start and end times for each piece of furniture must be within a certain limit, 
- and the end time for each piece of furniture is the start time plus the time it takes to move it.
The goal is to minimize the overall end time (end_time) for all the furniture, which means finding the most efficient way to move all the furniture with the available people.
