name: Solitaire Battleship
description: The Solitaire Battleship problem is a single-player puzzle where the goal is to find the locations of different types of ships (battleship, cruisers, destroyers, and submarines) on a 10x10 grid, given some hints about their locations. The hints are represented by different symbols, each indicating a specific type of ship or its orientation.
variables: $grid_{row,col}$, where $row \in GRID\_RANGE$ and $col \in GRID\_RANGE$. These are decision variables that represent whether a ship is present (1) or not (0) at a given position on the grid.
constraints: 
$\forall row \in GRID\_RANGE: \sum_{col \in GRID\_RANGE} grid_{row,col} = row\_tallies_{row}$, 
$\forall col \in GRID\_RANGE: \sum_{row \in GRID\_RANGE} grid_{row,col} = column\_tallies_{col}$, 
$(hint_{row,col} = 0) \rightarrow (grid_{row,col} = 0)$, 
$(hint_{row,col} = 1) \rightarrow (grid_{row,col} = 1 \wedge \forall (row',col') \in neighbors(row,col): grid_{row',col'} = 0)$, 
$(hint_{row,col} = 2) \rightarrow (grid_{row,col} = 1 \wedge ((grid_{row-1,col} = 1 \wedge grid_{row+1,col} = 1) \vee (grid_{row,col-1} = 1 \wedge grid_{row,col+1} = 1)))$, 
$(hint_{row,col} = 3) \rightarrow (grid_{row,col} = 1 \wedge grid_{row+1,col} = 1)$, 
$(hint_{row,col} = 4) \rightarrow (grid_{row,col} = 1 \wedge grid_{row-1,col} = 1)$, 
$(hint_{row,col} = 5) \rightarrow (grid_{row,col} = 1 \wedge grid_{row,col-1} = 1)$, 
$(hint_{row,col} = 6) \rightarrow (grid_{row,col} = 1 \wedge grid_{row,col+1} = 1)$. 
These constraints ensure that the number of ships in each row and column matches the given tallies, and that the hints are respected.
