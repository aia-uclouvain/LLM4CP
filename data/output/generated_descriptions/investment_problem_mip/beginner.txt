Investment Problem
The problem is about a person who manages money and wants to invest it in different opportunities to get the most return. 
The main variables are the amounts invested in each opportunity and the total return. 
The basic restrictions are that the total amount invested cannot exceed the budget of $100 million, and each opportunity has a limited amount that can be invested, ranging from $0 to $40 million. 
The goal is to maximize the total annual return without exceeding the budget.
