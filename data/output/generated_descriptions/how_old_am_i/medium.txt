The problem is known as the "How Old Am I" puzzle. 
It is about determining the current ages of two individuals, the narrator and their brother, based on two given conditions related to their ages at different points in time. 
The main decision variables are "me" and "him", which represent the current ages of the narrator and their brother, respectively. 
The key constraints are that six years ago, the brother was twice as old as the narrator, and in five years, the sum of their ages will be 40. 
The problem's objective is to find a solution that satisfies these conditions, rather than minimizing or maximizing a specific value, as it is a satisfaction problem where the goal is to find values for "me" and "him" that make the constraints true.
