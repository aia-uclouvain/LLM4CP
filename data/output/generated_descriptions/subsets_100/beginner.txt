Subsets 100 puzzle
The problem is about finding two groups of numbers from a given set of 10 different numbers between 1 and 100, such that the sum of the numbers in each group is the same.
The main variables are: 
- s and t, which are the two groups of numbers
- s_total and t_total, which are the sums of the numbers in each group.
The basic restrictions or rules are: 
- The two groups must not overlap, meaning they cannot have any numbers in common
- Each group must have at least one number
- The two groups together can have at most 10 numbers
- The sum of the numbers in each group must be the same.
The goal of the problem is to prove that it is always possible to find two such groups, by finding an example that satisfies all the rules.
