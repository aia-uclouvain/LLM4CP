3 Jugs Problem
The problem is about finding the shortest way to get from one point to another in a network of connected points. 
The main variables are the distance between points, the flow of "stuff" between points, and the total cost of getting from the start to the end.
The basic restrictions are that you can only move between connected points, and you can't move more "stuff" than the connection can handle. 
The goal is to minimize the total cost of getting from the start to the end, which in this case is limited to 7 units.
