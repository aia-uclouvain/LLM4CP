Movie theater seating problem. 
This problem is about arranging couples in a row of seats at a movie theater. 
The main variables are the seats, represented by the array x, which means each person's position in the row. 
The basic restrictions are that each person, except those at the ends, must sit next to someone of the same gender or their spouse. 
The goal of the problem is to find a valid arrangement of the couples in the row, following the given restrictions.
