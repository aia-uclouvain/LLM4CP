Evens Puzzle
The problem is about placing a certain number of chips on a 4x4 grid so that each row and each column has an even number of chips.
The main variables are the positions on the grid, where each position can either have a chip (represented by 1) or not have a chip (represented by 0).
The basic restrictions are that the total number of chips on the grid must be 10, and each row and each column must have an even number of chips.
The goal of the problem is to find a way to place the chips on the grid that satisfies these restrictions, with no specific goal to minimize or maximize anything, just to find a valid solution.
