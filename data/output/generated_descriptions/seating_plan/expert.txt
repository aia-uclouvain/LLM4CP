name: Seating Plan Problem
description: The problem involves arranging 20 candidates, 4 from each of 5 different languages (Norwegian, German, English, French, and Spanish), in a 6x6 grid such that no two candidates from the same language are adjacent, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, and each language has exactly 4 candidates.
variables: $x_{i,j}$, where $i$ and $j$ are the row and column indices, respectively. These variables represent the language of the candidate seated at position $(i,j)$ in the grid, with $x_{i,j} = 0$ indicating an empty seat and $x_{i,j} \in \{1,2,3,4,5\}$ indicating a candidate from one of the 5 languages.
constraints: $\forall i,j \in \{1,\ldots,6\}: x_{i,j} = 0 \lor x_{i,j} > 0$, $\forall i \in \{1,\ldots,6\}: \text{all\_different}(\{x_{i,j} | j \in \{1,\ldots,6\}, \text{data}_{i,j} = 1\})$, $\forall i \in \{1,\ldots,6\}: \text{all\_different}(\{x_{j,i} | j \in \{1,\ldots,6\}, \text{data}_{j,i} = 1\})$, $\forall i,j \in \{2,\ldots,5\}: \forall a,b \in \{-1,0,1\}: x_{i+a,j+b} \neq x_{i,j}$ if $\text{data}_{i,j} = 1$ and $(a,b) \neq (0,0)$, $\text{global\_cardinality}(\{x_{i,j} | i,j \in \{1,\ldots,6\}, \text{data}_{i,j} = 1\}, [4,4,4,4,4])$. These constraints ensure that each row and column contains distinct languages, that no two candidates from the same language are adjacent, and that each language has exactly 4 candidates.
objective: satisfy, meaning the goal is to find a feasible arrangement of candidates that satisfies all the constraints, rather than minimizing or maximizing a specific objective function.
