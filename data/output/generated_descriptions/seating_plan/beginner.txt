Seating Plan Problem
The problem is about arranging students in an examination room to prevent cheating. 
The main variables are the seats in the examination room, represented by a grid where each cell can hold a student taking a specific subject (Norwegian, German, English, French, or Spanish).
The basic restrictions are that students taking the same subject cannot be near each other, not even diagonally, and there must be exactly 4 students for each subject. 
The goal is to find all possible arrangements that satisfy these conditions, which means finding all the different ways to seat the students while preventing cheating.
