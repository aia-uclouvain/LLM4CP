constraints: $powerset(n, a) \wedge increasing(a)$, where $powerset(n, a)$ ensures that $a$ is a power set of the set with n elements, and $increasing(a)$ is a symmetry-breaking constraint that orders the subsets in $a$.
objective: The objective of the problem is to satisfy the constraints, i.e., to generate all possible subsets of the set with n elements, rather than minimizing or maximizing a specific value.
