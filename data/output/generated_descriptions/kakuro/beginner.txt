Kakuro puzzle
The problem is about filling in numbers in a grid to satisfy certain rules. 
The main variables are the numbers that go in each cell of the grid, which can be a digit from 1 to 9. 
The basic restrictions are that each row and column of cells must add up to a certain number, and no digit can be repeated in any row or column. 
The goal is to find a solution that satisfies all these rules, filling in all the blank cells with numbers from 1 to 9.
