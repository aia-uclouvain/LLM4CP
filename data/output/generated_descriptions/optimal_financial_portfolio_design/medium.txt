Optimal Pairwise Design (OPD) 
The Optimal Pairwise Design problem is about finding a binary matrix with specific properties. 
The main decision variables are the elements of the binary matrix, where each element represents whether a particular asset is included in a particular portfolio. 
The key constraints are that each row (representing a portfolio) must have a specified number of assets, and the dot product between any pair of distinct rows must be minimal. 
The objective is to minimize the maximum dot product between all pairs of distinct rows, which represents the minimum similarity between any two portfolios.
