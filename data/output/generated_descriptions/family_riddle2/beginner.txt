Family Riddle 
The problem is about two families, Leo's and Rene's, each having three girls and three boys. 
The main variables are the ages of the children in each family, which are represented by leo_boys, leo_girls, rene_boys, and rene_girls. These variables mean the ages of the boys and girls in each family, with each age being a whole number from 0 to 9. 
The basic restrictions are that all children in each family are of different ages, the youngest child in each family is a girl, Rene has a newborn girl, and the sum of the ages of the girls is equal to the sum of the ages of the boys in each family. Also, the total sum of the ages of all children is 60. 
The goal of the problem is to find the ages of the children in these two families that satisfy all the given conditions.
