Popsicle Stand Problem
The problem is about managing the staff of a Popsicle stand to minimize weekly staffing costs while meeting the required staffing levels.
The main variables are: 
- full_time: the number of full-time employees working on each day, 
- part_time: the number of part-time employees working on each day, 
- all_f and all_p: the total number of full-time and part-time employees working on each day, 
- z: the total weekly staffing cost.
The basic restrictions are: 
- full-time employees work five consecutive days, 
- part-time employees work two consecutive days, 
- the total number of employees working on each day must meet the required demand.
The goal is to minimize the total weekly staffing cost, z, which is calculated based on the number of full-time and part-time employees working each day, with full-time employees earning $100 per day and part-time employees earning $150 per day.
