The Popsicle Stand Staffing problem. 
This problem is about determining the minimum weekly staffing cost for a Popsicle stand while meeting the required staffing levels. 
The main decision variables are `full_time` and `part_time`, which represent the number of full-time and part-time employees starting work on each day of the week. 
The key constraints are that the total number of employees working on each day must meet the required demand, and that full-time employees work five consecutive days and part-time employees work two consecutive days. 
The objective is to minimize the total weekly staffing cost, which is calculated based on the number of full-time and part-time employees and their respective daily wages.
