N-Queens Problem
The problem is about placing queens on a chessboard in such a way that no queen attacks another.
The main variables are the positions of the queens on the board, which can be represented by their row and column numbers.
The basic restrictions or rules of the problem are that no two queens can be in the same row, column, or diagonal.
The goal of the problem is to find a placement of queens on the board such that all these restrictions are satisfied, which is known as a solution to the N-Queens problem.
