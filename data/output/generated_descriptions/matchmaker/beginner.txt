Matchmaker problem
The problem is about finding pairs of men and women who have at least one trait in common, such as hair color or eye color. 
The main variables are the women and men, each with their respective hair colors and eye colors. 
The basic restrictions are that each man can only be paired with one woman, and each woman can only be paired with one man. Also, a man and a woman can only be paired if they have at least one trait in common. 
The goal of the problem is to find all possible pairings of men and women where everyone has a partner with at least one trait in common.
