The 0/1 Knapsack Problem. 
This problem is about packing a bag with a limited capacity in the best way possible. 
The main variables are the items to pack, their weights, and their ratings, which represent how much we like each item. 
The basic restrictions are that we can't pack more than the bag can hold and we can either pack an item or not, we can't pack a fraction of it. 
The goal is to pack the items in a way that maximizes the total rating, or how much we like the items we packed, without exceeding the bag's capacity.
