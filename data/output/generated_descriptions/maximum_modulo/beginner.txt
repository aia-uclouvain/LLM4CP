Maximum Modulo Problem
The problem is about comparing a list of numbers and finding the one that has the largest remainder when divided by a certain number.
The main variables are: 
- variables: a list of numbers to be compared
- t_max: the number with the largest remainder
- m: the number by which the remainders are calculated
The basic restrictions are: 
- each number in the list must be between 1 and 9
- the remainder of each number when divided by m must be calculated
- the number with the largest remainder must be found
The goal is to find the number t_max that has the largest remainder when divided by m, and to verify that this number is indeed 5 when the list of numbers is [9,1,7,6,5] and m is 3.
