Blending problem
The problem is about creating a mixture of metals by combining different raw materials and scraps, while also using some already processed ingots. 
The main variables are: 
p, the amount of pure metals used, 
r, the amount of raw materials used, 
s, the amount of scraps used, 
i, the amount of ingots used, 
metal, the amount of each metal in the final mixture, 
and z, the total cost of creating the mixture.
The basic restrictions are: 
each metal must be within a certain percentage of the final mixture, 
the total amount of the mixture must be 71 units, 
and the amount of each raw material, scrap, and ingot used cannot be negative. 
The goal is to minimize the total cost, z, of creating the mixture.
