Maximum Cut Problem
The problem is about dividing a network of connected points into two groups in a way that maximizes the number of connections between points in different groups.
The main variables are: 
- x[i] which represents whether a point i is in group 1 or group 2, 
- E[i,1] and E[i,2] which represent the two points connected by a line i, 
- w[i] which is the weight or importance of the connection between points E[i,1] and E[i,2], 
- z which is the total weight of connections between points in different groups.
The basic restrictions are: 
- each point can only be in one group, 
- the weight of connections between points in the same group does not count, 
- the weight of connections between points in different groups should be maximized.
The goal is to maximize z, the total weight of connections between points in different groups.
