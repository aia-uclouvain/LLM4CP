Calvin Puzzle
The Calvin puzzle is about filling a 10x10 grid with numbers from 1 to 100 in a specific order. 
The main variables are the numbers in the grid, which represent the order in which the numbers are placed. 
The basic restrictions are that each number can only be placed a certain distance away from the previous number, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, with specific gaps in between. 
The goal is to fill the entire grid with numbers from 1 to 100, following these movement rules.
