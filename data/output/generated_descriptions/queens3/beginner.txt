N Queens Problem
The problem is about placing queens on a chessboard in such a way that no queen can attack another queen. 
The main variables are the positions of the queens on the board, which are represented by the "queens" array, where each number in the array shows the row where a queen is placed in a specific column.
The basic restrictions are that each queen must be in a different row, and no queen can be on the same diagonal as another queen. 
The goal of the problem is to find a way to place all the queens on the board so that they do not attack each other, and the model will output one possible solution.
