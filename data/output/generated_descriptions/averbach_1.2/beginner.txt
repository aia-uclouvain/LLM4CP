The problem is called "Hearts Game Problem". 
It's about three women - an American, an Englishwoman, and a Frenchwoman - playing a card game around a circular table. 
The main variables are the women's names (American, English, French) and their positions at the table (X, Y, Z), which represent who is sitting where. 
The basic rules are that each woman passes cards to the person on her right, and some specific cards were passed between them, like the queen of spades and hearts. 
The goal is to figure out who is the American, the Englishwoman, and the Frenchwoman, based on the information about the cards they passed to each other.
