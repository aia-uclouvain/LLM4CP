Facility Location problem.
The problem is about a company that wants to open warehouses in different cities to ship products to various regions. The company needs to decide which warehouses to open and how many products to ship from each warehouse to meet the demand in each region while keeping costs as low as possible.

The main variables are: 
- open: whether a warehouse is open or not
- ships: how many products to ship from each warehouse to each region
- tot_cost: the total cost of opening warehouses and shipping products

The basic restrictions or rules are: 
- each warehouse can only ship a certain number of products per week
- the demand in each region must be met
- if a warehouse is open, it must ship some products
- if the New York warehouse is open, the Los Angeles warehouse must also be open
- no more than three warehouses can be open
- either the Atlanta or the Los Angeles warehouse must be open

The goal of the problem is to minimize the total cost of opening warehouses and shipping products to meet the demand in each region.
