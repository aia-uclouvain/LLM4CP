The problem is called "Next Element". 
It's about finding a specific item in a list of items that comes after a certain point. 
The main variables are: 
- "threshold" which is like a starting point, 
- "ind" which is the position of the item we're looking for, 
- "table_vars" which is the list of items, and 
- "val" which is the specific item we want to find. 
The basic rules are that "ind" must be greater than "threshold", and "ind" must be the smallest position in the list where we can find "val". 
The goal is to check if "ind" is the correct position of "val" in the list, given the "threshold" starting point.
