Shopping Basket Optimization Problem
The problem is about finding the best way to buy a list of items from different shops to minimize the total cost. 
The main variables are the items to buy, the shops to buy from, and the total cost. 
The basic restrictions are that each item can only be bought from one shop, and each shop has a delivery cost that is added to the total cost if the total cost of items from that shop is below a certain limit. 
The goal is to minimize the total cost, which includes the cost of the items and the delivery costs.
