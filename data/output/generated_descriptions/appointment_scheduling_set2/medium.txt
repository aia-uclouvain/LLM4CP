Appointment Scheduling Problem
The problem involves scheduling interviews between an employer and N people, with each person having a free-busy schedule for N interview slots. 
The main decision variables are the assignments of persons to a slot, represented by the array `x`, where `x[i]` denotes the slot assigned to person `i`. 
The key constraints are that each person can only be assigned to a slot that they are free, and each slot can only be assigned to one person, ensured by the `alldifferent` constraint. 
The objective of the problem is to find a feasible assignment of persons to slots, if one exists, which is achieved by solving the `satisfy` problem.
