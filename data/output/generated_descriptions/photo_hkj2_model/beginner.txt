Photo Problem
The problem is about arranging people in a line for a photo, taking into account their preferences for standing next to specific others.
The main variables are: 
- n, the number of people in the photo
- num_pref, the number of preferences for standing next to someone
- preferences, a list of pairs of people who want to stand next to each other
- positions, the arrangement of people in the line
- z, a measure of how many preferences are satisfied.
The basic restrictions are that each person can only be in one position, and everyone must be in the line. 
The goal is to maximize z, which means satisfying as many preferences as possible for people to stand next to each other.
