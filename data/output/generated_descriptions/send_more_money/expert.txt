name: Send More Money Problem
description: The Send More Money problem is a classic puzzle where the goal is to assign digits to letters in a mathematical equation such that the equation holds true. The equation is "SEND + MORE = MONEY", where each letter represents a unique digit from 0 to 9.
variables: S, E, N, D, M, O, R, Y - These variables represent the digits assigned to each letter in the equation, where S, E, N, D, M, O, R, and Y are the digits for the letters S, E, N, D, M, O, R, and Y, respectively.
constraints: all_different(fd) ∧ 1000*S + 100*E + 10*N + D + 1000*M + 100*O + 10*R + E = 10000*M + 1000*O + 100*N + 10*E + Y ∧ S > 0 ∧ M > 0 - These constraints ensure that each letter is assigned a unique digit, the equation "SEND + MORE = MONEY" holds true, and the letters S and M are not assigned the digit 0, as they are the leading digits in the numbers.
objective: satisfy - The objective of the problem is to find an assignment of digits to letters that satisfies all the constraints, rather than minimizing or maximizing a specific value.
