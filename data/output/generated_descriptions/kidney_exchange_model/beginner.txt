Kidney Exchange Problem
The problem is about matching people who need a kidney with people who can donate a kidney, in a way that maximizes the number of successful exchanges.
The main variables are: 
- num_people: the total number of people involved
- compatible: a list of sets, where each set contains the people who can donate a kidney to a particular person
- x: a list of variables, where x[i] represents the person who donates a kidney to person i
- z: the total number of successful kidney exchanges.
The basic restrictions are: 
- a person can only receive a kidney from someone who is compatible with them
- a person can only donate a kidney to someone who is compatible with them
- a person cannot donate a kidney to themselves
- if person A donates a kidney to person B, then person B cannot donate a kidney to person A.
The goal is to maximize the number of successful kidney exchanges, which is represented by the variable z.
