Send More Money problem. 
This problem is about finding the largest possible amount of money that can be sent using a mathematical equation where each letter represents a digit from 0 to 9. 
The main variables are S, E, N, D, M, O, T, and Y, which represent the digits in the words "SEND" and "MONEY". 
The basic restrictions are that each letter must be a different digit, and the equation "SEND + MOST = MONEY" must be true, with S and M being non-zero. 
The goal is to maximize the value of "MONEY", which means finding the largest possible amount that can be sent.
