Secret Santa problem
The problem is about a gift exchange game where each person draws another person's name and buys a gift for them. The goal is to make sure that nobody buys a gift for their spouse or someone they bought a gift for recently.
The main variables are: 
- "santas" which represents the person each individual will buy a gift for
- "santa_distance" which represents how long it has been since each person last bought a gift for the person they are assigned to this year
- "spouses" which represents the spouse of each person
- "rounds" which represents the history of who each person has bought gifts for in the past.
The basic restrictions are: 
- A person cannot buy a gift for their spouse
- A person cannot buy a gift for someone they bought a gift for last year
- Each person can only buy a gift for one other person.
The goal of the problem is to maximize the "distance" since each person last bought a gift for the person they are assigned to this year, which means trying to make sure that each person buys a gift for someone they haven't bought a gift for in as long as possible.
