Divisible by Unit Digits problem. 
This problem is about finding numbers between 10 and 50 that can be divided evenly by their last digit. 
The main variables are the numbers between 10 and 50, and a special variable that tracks whether each number has the property of being divisible by its last digit. 
The basic restriction is that a number can only be considered if it can be divided evenly by its last digit, for example, 12 can be divided by 2, but 13 cannot be divided by 3. 
The goal of the problem is to count how many numbers between 10 and 50 have this property.
