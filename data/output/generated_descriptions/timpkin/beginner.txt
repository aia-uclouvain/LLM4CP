Mrs Timpkin's Age problem 
is about figuring out how old Mrs. Timpkin is, given some information about her age and her husband's age. 
The main variables are T, which is Mr. Timpkin's current age, and W, which is Mrs. Timpkin's current age. 
The basic restrictions are that eighteen years ago, Mr. Timpkin was three times as old as his wife, and now he is twice as old as she. 
The goal is to find Mrs. Timpkin's current age, which means finding the value of W that satisfies these conditions.
