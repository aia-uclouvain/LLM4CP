The problem is called the "Message Passing Problem". 
It's about a teacher who wants to send a message to a student named Robert through other students. 
The main variables are the students and the order in which they meet each other. 
The students are represented by names like Craig, Jason, and Robert, and the order they meet is like a path or a chain of meetings. 
The basic restrictions are that each meeting between students must be possible according to the given information, and the message must eventually reach Robert. 
The goal is to find the shortest path, or chain of meetings, that will get the message from the teacher to Robert.
