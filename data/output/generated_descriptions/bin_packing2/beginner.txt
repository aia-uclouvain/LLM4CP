Bin Packing Problem
The problem is about packing items of different weights into bins with a limited capacity, so that each item is assigned to a bin and the total weight of items in each bin does not exceed the capacity.
The main variables are: 
- bins: which bin each item is assigned to
- weights: the weight of each item
- capacity: the maximum weight a bin can hold
The basic restrictions are: 
- each item must be assigned to a bin
- the total weight of items in each bin cannot exceed the capacity
The goal is to find a way to assign items to bins that satisfies these restrictions, which is known as a "feasible solution".
