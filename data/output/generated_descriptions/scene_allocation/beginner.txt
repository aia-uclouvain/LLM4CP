Scene Allocation Problem
The problem is about scheduling the filming of scenes for a movie. 
The main variables are: 
- shoot: the day when each scene will be filmed
- isPaid: whether each actor is paid on each day
- isShot: whether each scene is filmed on each day
The basic restrictions are: 
- each scene can only be filmed once
- at most 5 scenes can be filmed per day
- all actors in a scene must be present on the day the scene is filmed
- each actor has a daily fee that must be paid if they are present
The goal is to minimize the total production cost by finding the best schedule for filming the scenes.
