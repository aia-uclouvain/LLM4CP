Job Shop Scheduling Problem
The problem is about scheduling a series of tasks with specific dependencies and durations to minimize the total time required to complete all tasks.
The main decision variables are Ta, Tb, Tc, Td, and Tf, which represent the completion times of tasks A, B, C, D, and F, respectively.
The key constraints are:
- Task B cannot start until 2 time units after Task A is completed.
- Task C cannot start until 2 time units after Task A is completed.
- Task D cannot start until 3 time units after Task B is completed.
- Task F cannot start until 5 time units after Task C is completed and 4 time units after Task D is completed.
The objective of the problem is to minimize the completion time of Task F (Tf), which represents the total time required to complete all tasks.
