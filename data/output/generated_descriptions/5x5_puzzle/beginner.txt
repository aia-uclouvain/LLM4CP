5x5 Puzzle 
The problem is about a game where you have a 5x5 grid of squares that can be either lit or unlit. When you click on a square, it and the squares next to it (up, down, left, right) will change their state (lit to unlit or unlit to lit). 
The main variables are the state of each square (x) and the number of times each square has been clicked (d), as well as the total number of clicks (the_sum). 
The basic rules are that when a square is clicked, it and its neighbors change state, and each click counts as one move. 
The goal is to light all 25 squares in the fewest number of clicks (minimize the_sum).
