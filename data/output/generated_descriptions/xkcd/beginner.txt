xkcd Knapsack problem 
This problem is about ordering food from a menu to reach a specific total cost of $15.05. 
The main variables are the prices of each dish and how many of each dish to order. 
The basic restrictions are that the total cost of the ordered dishes must be exactly $15.05, and you can't order a negative number of dishes. 
The goal of the problem is to find a combination of dishes that adds up to the total cost of $15.05.
