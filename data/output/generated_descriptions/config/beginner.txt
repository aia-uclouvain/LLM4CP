Config Problem
The problem is about configuring equipment in racks to minimize the total cost while meeting certain requirements.
The main variables are: 
- rack: which model of equipment is placed in each rack
- counters: how many of each type of card is placed in each rack
- cost: the total cost of the configuration
The basic restrictions or rules are: 
- each rack has a limited amount of power and connectors
- each type of card has a specific power requirement and quantity
- all cards must be placed in the racks
The goal is to minimize the total cost of the configuration by choosing the right equipment models and card placements.
