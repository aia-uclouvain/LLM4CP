Seseman's Convent Problem
The problem is about filling in numbers in a square grid, where the numbers on the edges of the grid must add up to the same total, and all the numbers inside the grid are zero. 
The main variables are the size of the grid (n), the total sum of all numbers in the grid (total_sum), and the numbers in each cell of the grid (x). 
The basic restrictions are that the numbers on each edge of the grid must add up to the same total (border_sum), all the numbers inside the grid must be zero, and each number on the edge must be at least 1. 
The goal of the problem is to find a set of numbers that satisfy all these restrictions, and to determine the total sum of all numbers in the grid.
