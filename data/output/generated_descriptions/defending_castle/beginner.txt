Defending the Castle puzzle 
The problem is about defending a square castle with a certain number of fighters. 
The main variables are p and q, which represent the number of defenders in different positions around the castle. 
The basic restrictions are that the total number of defenders is 28, and the number of defenders on each side of the castle should be as high as possible, with p and q being non-negative and q being at least as many as p. 
The goal is to maximize the number of defenders on each side of the castle.
