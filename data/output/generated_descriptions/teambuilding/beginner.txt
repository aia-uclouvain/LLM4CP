Teambuilding Problem
The problem is about organizing 10 teams of 6 people each from a group of 60 employees, with 30 new employees and 30 existing employees, to respect certain rules and constraints.

The main variables are the teams and the employees, where each employee is assigned to a team. The teams are numbered from 1 to 10, and the employees are numbered from 0 to 59, with even numbers representing new employees and odd numbers representing existing employees.

The basic restrictions or rules of the problem are:
- Each team must have 3 existing employees and 3 new employees.
- No more than 4 people from the same service (A to F) can be in the same team.
- Employees from services A and B cannot be in the same team, and employees from services E and F cannot be in the same team.
- A new employee who is coached by an existing employee must be in the same team as their coach.
- Certain employees have specific preferences about which team they want to be in, based on the person they want to be with.

The goal of the problem is to find a way to assign all 60 employees to the 10 teams while respecting all the given rules and constraints, without any specific objective to minimize or maximize. The solution will provide a satisfactory assignment of employees to teams.
