Student Interview Assignment Problem
The problem is about assigning students to companies for interviews, where each student has a preference for which companies they want to interview with. 
The main variables are: 
- Student preferences: a list of numbers showing how much each student likes each company
- Company assignments: a list of numbers showing which company each student is assigned to for an interview
The basic restrictions or rules of the problem are: 
- Each student can only interview with a certain number of companies
- Each company can only have a certain number of students interviewing with them
- Students should be assigned to companies they prefer, if possible
The goal of the problem is to minimize the total "regret" of the students, which means assigning students to their preferred companies as much as possible, while following the rules.
