Scheduling problem
This problem is about planning a schedule for a set of tasks that require different amounts of time and resources. 
The main variables are: 
- the start time of each task (called "origin"), 
- the end time of each task, 
- the total time it takes to complete all tasks (called "End"), 
- the duration of each task, and 
- the amount of resources each task requires.
The basic restrictions are: 
- each task must start after a certain time and take a specific amount of time, 
- the total amount of resources used at any time cannot exceed a certain limit, 
- and the end time of each task is calculated by adding its start time and duration.
The goal is to find a schedule that minimizes the total time it takes to complete all tasks, without using more resources than available.
