The problem is called the Circular Except 0 constraint. 
It's about arranging a sequence of numbers from 0 to 5 in a circle, following certain rules. 
The main variables are the numbers in the sequence, which are represented by the array x. 
These numbers can be thought of as the values we want to arrange in a circle. 
The basic restrictions or rules of the problem are that the sequence must follow a specific pattern defined by a set of transitions, and the number of zeros in the sequence must be related to the length of the sequence in a certain way. 
The goal of the problem is to find a sequence that satisfies all these rules, which is achieved by solving the problem as a satisfaction problem, meaning we just want to find a solution that meets all the constraints, without trying to minimize or maximize anything.
