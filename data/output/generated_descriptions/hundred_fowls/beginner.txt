Hundred Fowls Puzzle
The problem is about buying 100 birds with 100 coins, where each type of bird has a different price. 
The main variables are the number of cocks, hens, and chicks, which represent how many of each type of bird are bought.
The basic restrictions are that the total number of birds is 100, the total cost is 100 coins, and the number of chicks must be a multiple of 3 because they are sold in groups of three. 
The goal is to find how many cocks, hens, and chicks can be bought under these conditions.
