Magic Modulo Number problem. 
This problem is about finding a specific number that has a unique property when divided by other numbers. 
The main variable is "x", which is the number we're trying to find. 
The basic restrictions or rules of the problem are that when "x" is divided by numbers from 2 to 10, the remainder is always one less than the number it's being divided by. 
The goal of the problem is to find the smallest possible "x" that satisfies all these rules.
