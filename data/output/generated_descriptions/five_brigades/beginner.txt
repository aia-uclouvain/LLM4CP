The Five Brigades puzzle 
is about five men - Alfonso, Benito, Carlos, Diego, and Esteban - who have stolen 200 gold coins and are trying to figure out how many each of them has. 
The main variables are A, B, C, D2, and E3, which represent the number of gold coins each man has. 
The basic restrictions are that each man must have at least 8 coins and no more than 160 coins, and if they had a certain multiple of coins, they would still have 200 coins in total. 
The goal is to find all possible combinations of coins that the men could have, with the condition that every man has a whole number of coins and no one has fractional coins.
