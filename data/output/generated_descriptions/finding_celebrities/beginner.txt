Finding Celebrities problem
The problem is about finding people at a party who are known by everyone else, but only know other people who are also known by everyone else.
The main variables are: 
- the number of people at the party (n), 
- the relationships between people at the party (graph), 
- the people each person knows (party), 
- the set of celebrities (celebrities), 
- the number of celebrities (num_celebrities).
The basic restrictions are: 
- everyone knows themselves, 
- if a person is a celebrity, everyone else must know them, 
- if a person is a celebrity, they can only know other celebrities.
The goal is to find the set of celebrities at the party, which are people who are known by everyone else and only know other celebrities.
