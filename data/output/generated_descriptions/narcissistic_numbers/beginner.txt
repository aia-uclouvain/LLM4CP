Narcissistic Numbers problem.
This problem is about finding special numbers that are equal to the sum of a certain power of their digits. 
The main variables are: 
- n, which is the number of digits in the number, 
- x, which is an array representing the digits of the number, 
- num, which is the number itself, 
- base, which is the base of the number system (like 10 for decimal).
The basic restrictions are: 
- the number must be greater than 0, 
- the first digit must be greater than 0, 
- the number must be equal to the sum of the nth power of its digits.
The goal of the problem is to find a number that satisfies these conditions, specifically a number that is equal to the sum of the nth power of its digits.
