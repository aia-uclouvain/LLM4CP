name: Movie Theater Seating Problem

description: The problem involves seating n couples in a row such that each person, except those at the ends, has two neighbors who are either of the same gender or the spouse of the person. The goal is to find all possible seating arrangements that satisfy this condition.

variables: x = (x[1], x[2], ..., x[m]), gender = (gender[1], gender[2], ..., gender[m]) 
These are decision variables where x[i] represents the person seated at position i, and gender[i] represents the gender of the person seated at position i, with 0 denoting male (husband) and 1 denoting female (wife).

constraints: 
alldifferent(x), 
gender[i] = x[i] mod 2, 
(gender[i] = gender[i-1]) ∨ is_couple(n, x[i], x[i-1], couples) 
These constraints ensure that each position is occupied by a unique person, the gender of each person is determined by their seat number, and each person's neighbors are either of the same gender or their spouse.

objective: The problem is a satisfaction problem, meaning the objective is to find all possible seating arrangements that satisfy the given constraints, rather than minimizing or maximizing a specific value.
