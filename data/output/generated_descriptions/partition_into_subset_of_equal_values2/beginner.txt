Partition Problem
The problem is about dividing a set of numbers into two or more subsets, where the sum of the numbers in each subset is equal.
The main variables are: 
- `s`: the set of numbers to be divided
- `x`: a decision variable that determines which subset each number belongs to
- `num_subsets`: the number of subsets to divide the numbers into
- `ssum`: the target sum for each subset, calculated by dividing the total sum of the numbers by the number of subsets
The basic restrictions or rules of the problem are: 
- The sum of the numbers in each subset must be equal
- The numbers can only be divided into the specified number of subsets
- The sum of the numbers must be divisible by the number of subsets
The goal of the problem is to find a way to divide the numbers into subsets with equal sums, if possible.
