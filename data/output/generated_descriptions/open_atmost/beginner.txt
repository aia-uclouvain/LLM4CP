Open Atmost Problem
The problem is about limiting the number of times a certain value appears in a specific part of a list. 
The main variables are: 
- a list of numbers (called "variables"), 
- a specific part of the list (called "s"), 
- a maximum number (called "n"), 
- and a target value (called "value"). 
The basic restrictions are that the list must have a certain length and the specific part of the list must be within that list. 
The goal is to ensure that the target value appears at most a certain number of times in the specific part of the list.
