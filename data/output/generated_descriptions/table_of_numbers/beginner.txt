Table of Numbers Problem
The problem is about selecting 5 numbers from a 5x5 table in such a way that no two numbers are from the same row or column.
The main variables are the numbers in the table (t) and the selection of numbers (x), which means choosing a number from each row and column without repeating any row or column.
The basic restrictions are that each row and each column can only be used once, and the goal is to find the sum of the selected numbers (the_sum).
The goal of the problem is to find the sum of the 5 selected numbers, which is represented by the variable the_sum, by choosing the numbers in such a way that satisfies the restrictions.
