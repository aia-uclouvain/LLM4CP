Transportation Problem
The problem is about delivering goods from factories to customers in the cheapest way possible. 
The main variables are: 
- the number of factories (num_plants) and customers (num_clients), 
- how much each factory can produce (capacity), 
- how much each customer needs (demand), 
- the cost of sending goods from each factory to each customer (cost), 
- how much is actually sent from each factory to each customer (x).
The basic restrictions are: 
- factories cannot send more goods than they can produce, 
- customers must receive exactly the amount of goods they need, 
- goods cannot be sent for free or for a negative cost.
The goal is to minimize the total cost of delivering all the goods to the customers.
