Color Sequence Optimization Problem
The problem is about arranging colors in a sequence to minimize changes between them. 
The main variables are: 
- colors: the original list of colors
- perm: the order in which the colors should be arranged
- optimal: the resulting list of colors after arrangement
The basic restrictions are: 
- each color in the optimal list must come from the original colors list
- the order of colors is determined by the perm list
The goal is to find the optimal arrangement that minimizes color changes, and to ensure that if two colors are the same, their order in the perm list is consistent.
