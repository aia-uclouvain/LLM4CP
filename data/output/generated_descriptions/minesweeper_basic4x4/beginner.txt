Minesweeper problem
The problem is about a game where you have to figure out where mines are hidden in a grid. 
The main variables are the grid cells, which can either have a mine (represented by X) or not have a mine (represented by 0 or 1, where 1 means a safe cell with a number indicating how many mines are nearby).
The basic restrictions are that each cell can either have a mine or not, and the numbers in the safe cells must match the number of mines surrounding them. 
The goal is to correctly identify which cells have mines and which do not, based on the given numbers in the safe cells.
