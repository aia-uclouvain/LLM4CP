Ramsey Problem
The problem is about coloring the connections between people at a party so that you don't have a certain number of people who all know each other or a certain number of people who all don't know each other.
The main variables are the colors of the connections between people, which are represented by numbers.
The basic restrictions are that each connection between two people can only have one color, and you can't have a group of people where everyone knows each other or a group of people where no one knows anyone else, all with the same color.
The goal is to find the smallest party where this is impossible, or in the given models, to minimize the maximum color used, which means finding a way to color the connections with the smallest number of colors possible without having a monochromatic group of people who all know or don't know each other.
