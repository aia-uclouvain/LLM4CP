The problem is called Contiguity. 
It's about arranging a series of 0s and 1s in a line so that all the 1s are together. 
The main variable is "arr", which is a list of 0s and 1s. 
The basic rule is that all the 1s in the list must be next to each other, and the rest of the list can be filled with 0s. 
The goal is to find a way to arrange the 0s and 1s that satisfies this rule.
