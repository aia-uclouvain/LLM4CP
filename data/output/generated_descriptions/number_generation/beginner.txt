The problem is called "Number Generation" or "Devil's Word". 
It is about finding a way to combine a list of numbers using basic math operations like addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division to get a specific total. 
The main variables are "x" which is a list of numbers, "ops" which is a list of operations to be performed between the numbers, and "total" which is the target result. 
The basic restrictions are that each operation can only be one of the four basic math operations, and the numbers and operations must be combined in a specific way to get the total. 
The goal is to find a combination of numbers and operations that adds up to the target total, which in this case is 666, and also matches a specific list of numbers that spell out a message.
