Collatz Conjecture problem 
The problem is about a sequence of numbers that starts with a given number and follows a simple rule to generate the next number in the sequence. 
The main variables are: n, which is the starting number, x, which is the sequence of numbers, and len, which is the length of the sequence until it reaches 1. 
The basic restrictions or rules of the problem are: if the current number is even, the next number is half of it, and if the current number is odd, the next number is 3 times the current number plus 1, divided by 2. The sequence must start with the given number n and must reach 1 at some point. 
The goal of the problem is to find the sequence of numbers that starts with n and follows the given rules, and to determine the length of this sequence until it reaches 1.
