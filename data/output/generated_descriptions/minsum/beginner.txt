Minsum problem
The problem is about finding the part of a list of numbers that has the smallest total sum. 
The main variables are: 
- start: the beginning position of the part of the list we are looking at
- end: the ending position of the part of the list we are looking at
- minsum: the smallest total sum of any part of the list
The basic restrictions or rules of the problem are: 
- the start position must be before or at the same position as the end position
- we are only looking at parts of the list that are next to each other, not parts that are separated
The goal of the problem is to minimize minsum, which means finding the part of the list with the smallest total sum.
