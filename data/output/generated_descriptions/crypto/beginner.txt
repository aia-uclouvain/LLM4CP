Crypto problem. 
This problem is about decoding a secret message where each letter of the alphabet is assigned a number from 1 to 26. 
The main variables are the letters A to Z, which represent the numbers assigned to each letter. 
The basic restrictions are that each letter must be assigned a unique number, and the sum of the numbers for each word must match the given total. 
The goal is to find the correct assignment of numbers to letters that satisfies all the given equations, such as BALLET equaling 45, and so on.
