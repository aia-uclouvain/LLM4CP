**Maximum Path Sum in a Triangle**
The problem is about finding the maximum total from top to bottom of a triangle, where each step can only move to adjacent numbers on the row below.

**Decision Variables**
The main decision variables are `x[i]`, which represent the column index of the number chosen at each row `i`. The variable `total` represents the maximum total sum that can be achieved.

**Constraints**
The constraints are:
- Each chosen number must be greater than 0.
- The difference between the column indices of two consecutive chosen numbers can be either 0 or 1, meaning we can only move to adjacent numbers on the row below.
- The first chosen number must be in the first column.
- The total sum is the sum of all chosen numbers.

**Objective**
The objective is to maximize the `total` sum, which means finding the path from top to bottom of the triangle that results in the largest sum of numbers.
